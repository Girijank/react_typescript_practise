"The Soldier and the Craftsman"

Roman soldier

Craftsman

Listen to and read the story below about a soldier who has just returned from war and comes to Rome to visit his friend, the craftsman.

Salve, mi amice! Deinde de illo bello longo rediisti!

Hello, my friend! You have finally returned from that long war!

Quid gaudium in mea corde te videre iterum habeo! Multi anni fuerunt.

What joy I have in my heart to see you again! It has been many years.

Istud bellum tibi crudele fuit. Species tua est misera. Corpus tuum multa vulnera habet. Dentes tuas amisisti. In vestibus sordidis vestis.

That war has been cruel to you. Your appearance is wretched. Your body has many wounds. You have lost your teeth. You dress in sordid clothes.

Etiam manus mea amisa est, et medicus sine arte eum in bracchium meum retro posuit.

Also, my hand was lost, and a doctor without skill put it back onto my arm backwards.

Id est ita crudele!

That is so cruel!
