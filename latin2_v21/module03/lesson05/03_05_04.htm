Latin Writing Assignment

In dialogue format, write three questions in Latin to find out about your patient's illness or injury. At least one question should contain an interrogative pronoun and at least one question should use an interrogative adjective.

Include the three responses to your questions, in English.

35–30 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • includes three complete question sentences in Latin (at least one question should contain an interrogative pronoun, and at least one question should use an interrogative adjective.)
  • includes three responses to the Latin questions in English
  • is formatted as a dialogue between the two characters described in previous steps

The sentences:

  • use a variety of vocabulary that fits the context of the story
  • consistently use correct grammar, syntax, and mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • are creative and demonstrate originality

Grammar:

Student uses all of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • sentence formatting of questions in Latin
  • interrogative pronouns
  • interrogative adjectives
  • subject-verb agreement
  • case endings
