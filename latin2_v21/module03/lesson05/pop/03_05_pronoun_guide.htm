Roman Virtues

Roman Virtues

Virtus

the courage or virtue a person possesses that brings great acclaim to them

Auctoritas

bestowed by the Senate upon a leader; the senses of ultimate authority

Clementia

mildness and gentleness

Dignitas

a sense of personal pride and self-worth

Honestas

the images that one presents as a respectable member of society

Pietas

the quality of being pious in respect to one’s religion, faith, and country

Humanitas

the refinement and sense of culture in an individual

Industria

the act of working hard in your profession

Veritas

having honesty when dealing with others

Prudens

showing wisdom in one’s actions
