Roman Virtues

Virtus

Virtue; the courage or virtue a person possesses that brings great acclaim to them
Auctoritas

Spiritual authority; bestowed by the Senate upon a leader; the senses of ultimate authority
Clementia

Mercy; mildness and gentleness
Dignitas

Dignity; a sense of personal pride and self-worth
Honestas

Respectibility; the images that one presents as a respectable member of society
Pietas

Piety; the quality of being pious in respect to one’s religion, faith, and country
Humanitas

Character; the refinement and sense of culture in an individual
Industria

Industrious; the act of working hard in your profession
Veritas

Truth; having honesty when dealing with others
Prudens

Sensible; showing wisdom in one’s actions
