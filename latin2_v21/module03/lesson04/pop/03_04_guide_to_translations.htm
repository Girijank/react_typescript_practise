Step-by-Step Guide to Translating a Latin Sentence (How to Translate Latin Sentences)

You now have many tools to translate longer Latin sentences. Here are quick tips to help guide you:

  • If you have an interrogative pronoun or adjective or a question word in the sentence, translate that first. Or if there is a question word in the sentence, translate that first.
