Vesta, Goddess of the Hearth

Vesta’s Power

Let’s listen to hear about the wonderful story of my origin and place in the pantheon!

I was the firstborn child of the Titans Saturn and Rhea. However, I was the last-born child that Saturn disgorged from his belly after Jupiter forced Saturn to release the children he swallowed. Apollo and Neptune asked to marry me, but I refused their proposals and remained loyal to my sacred duties as the goddess of the hearth. I protect the Roman family, hearth, and home with my loving comfort. Sum in meo templo cotidie maneo to ensure that I am always maintaining a steadfast level of security and warmth to my beloved Romans.
