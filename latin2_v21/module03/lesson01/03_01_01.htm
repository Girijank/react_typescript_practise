A Great Time to be Roman!

It’s a great time to be Roman! We are now in the middle of the Roman Republic, and my beloved Rome has become the most dominant city in the Mediterranean world. I care about my people so much that it warms my heart to know that by the end of the 3rd century BCE, the Romans had been able to:

  • conquer many foreign lands and Italian tribes
  • strengthen its army and naval power
  • increase its land mass
  • form treaties with conquered nations, and
  • establish a governmental structure that helped organize both the politics and people of Rome as a unified nation.
