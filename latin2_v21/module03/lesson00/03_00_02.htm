Collaboration Opportunities

You will have one collaboration opportunity in each segment of the course. It is recommended that you participate in the collaboration before you take your Segment Exam. Interacting with your classmates and instructor to discuss the content of the course will help you succeed in this class, and by practicing, you will improve your Latin.
