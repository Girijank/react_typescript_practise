Scipio family tree

Lucius Scipio Barbotus (Consul 298), his sons were Lucius Scipio (consul 259) and Gnaeus Scipio Asina (Consul 260, 254)–father of Publiys Scipio Asina (consul 221). Lucius Scipio had two sons, Gnaeus Scipio Calvus (Consul 222) and Publius Scipio (Consul 218). Gnaeus Scipio Calvus had two sons, Gnaeus Scipio Hispanallus (Consul 176 -no children) and Publius Scipio Nasica (consul 191), whose children were Publius Scipio Nasica Corculum (Consul 162, 155). Publius Scipio had two sons, Publius Scipio Africanus (consul 205, 194) and Lucius Scipio Asiaticus (consul 190–no children). Pulius Scipio Africanus had four children–Publius Scipio (who adopted Publius Scipio Aemilianus, Consul 147 and 138), Lucius Scipio, Cornelia (who married Publius Scipio Nasica, and had a son–Publius Scipio Nasica Serapio, Consul 138), and another daughter named Cornelia, who married Sempronius Gracchus.
