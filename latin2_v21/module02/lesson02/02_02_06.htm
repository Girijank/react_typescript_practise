The Twelve Tables

Translate these sentences and click on the arrow to reveal the answer.

In 451 BCE, prima classis decemvirorum decem tabulas legum Romanorum creavit. Eae leges iures civium inter alias cives demonstraverunt.

In 451 BCE, the first group of decemviri created ten tables of Roman laws.These laws demonstrated the rights of citizens between other citizens.

Postea secunda classis decemvirorum duo tabulas addidit; hae Duodecim Tabulae fuit aeniolae, et in Rostra apud Curiam in Foro Romano positae sunt, ubi omnes homines Romae videre potuerunt. Duodecim Tabulae leges ad iudicium de iento, poenis de detrimento casae aut rei familiaris ab hominibus aut animalibus, furto, funeribus, iuribus patris, patrimonioque demonstraverunt.

Later, the second group of decemviri added two tables. These Twelve Tables were bronze, and they were placed on the Rostra by the Curia in the Roman Forum, where all of the people of Rome were able to see. The Twelve Tables demonstrated the laws with respect to going to court, penalties for damage of home or property by people or animals, theft, funerals, rights of a father, and inheritance.

Plebes eas iures patriciorum in Rostra viderunt, et praesidium etiam postulaverunt. In 449 BCE, Duodecem Tabulae factae sunt leges officiales, et iures omnium civium, plebum patriciorumque, repraesentatae sunt.

The plebeians saw these rights of the patricians on the Rostra and demanded protection also. In 449 BCE, the Twelve Tables were made official laws, and the rights of all citizens, plebs and patricians were represented.

Cogitemus!

Let’s take a closer look at Table IX which contains the law: “For anyone whomsoever to be put to death without a trial and unconvicted...is forbidden.”

Now compare that to Bill of Rights, Amendment VI: In all criminal prosecutions, the accused shall enjoy the right to a speedy and public trial, by an impartial jury of the State and district wherein the crime shall have been committed, which district shall have been previously ascertained by law, and to be informed of the nature and cause of the accusation; to be confronted with the witnesses against him; to have compulsory process for obtaining witnesses in his favor, and to have the Assistance of Counsel for his defense.

Can you see the similarities between the two? Do you think the founding fathers of the United States based Amendment VI off Table IX of the Twelve Tables? While thinking of your response, you should know that many of the founding fathers were students of Latin and Greek.
