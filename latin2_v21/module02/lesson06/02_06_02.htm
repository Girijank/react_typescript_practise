Speaking Practice

Are you ready for a tricky treat? Take a listen to these tongue twisters I have crafted for you! When you are ready, give saying them a try. Remember, the more you speak Latin out loud, the more comfortable you will feel pronouncing and discussing the vocabulary and phrases you learn with your instructor.

If you can say these five times fast, you'll be ready for any pronunciation challenge thrown your way!

Patricius, qui puero pilam portare per portam pedibus percurrit, huic patrimonium dare paravit.

The patron, who dashed through the gate with his feet to bring a ball for the boy, prepared to give him an inheritance.
