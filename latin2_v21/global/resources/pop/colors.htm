More about colors (עוד על צבעים)

Print | Save

When you name a color without any reference to a noun, it always comes in the masculine and singular form. In other cases, the pronunciation of the color is dependent on whether the noun that precedes it is masculine, feminine, singular, or plural. The masculine/singular form serves as the basis for the other color name variation where only the suffix changes, but the root name remains the same.
