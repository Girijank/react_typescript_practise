When it is Polite to Point

You've probably been taught that it's rude to point at people. Well, hold on to your socks because you're about to get the go-ahead... sort of. In ASL, indexing is used to clarify pronouns. Being able to incorporate the physical space around you is the essence of using a visual language. In ASL, indexing lets everyone know exactly who is doing what and where.

Look at Chloe in the image below. She's really rockin' that orange shirt. Check out how she refers to the people around her at school.

Order of description:

In ASL, indexing descriptions aren't random, they occur in a specific order.


  1. Gender
  2. Ethnicity
  3. Tall, short, seated
  4. Body type
  5. Hair color, eye color, or clothing description
