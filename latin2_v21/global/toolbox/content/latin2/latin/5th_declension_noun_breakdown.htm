Nouns: 5th Declension

Dictionary Entry for a noun = Nominative singular form, Genitive singular, gender

  • dies, diei, f.—day

5th Declension nouns: Genitive singular ends in –ei.

Vocabulary Examples

  • dies, diei, m./f.—day
  • res, rei . f.—matter, thing

Forming and Declining the 5th Declension

Sentence Examples:

  1. Vir in media die venit. The man came in the middle of the day.
  2. Res Publica a duobos consulibus regebatur. The Republic was being ruled by two consuls.
  3. In ducibus nostris fidem habuimus. We had faith in our leaders.
