Irregular Adjectives and Adverbs

Irregular Adjectives: Some adjectives have their own unique form in the comparative and superlative degrees. You must memorize these forms. Follow the same declining rules of the comparative and superlative forms.

  • Comparative adjectives: Decline in the 3rd Declension.
  • Superlative adjectives: Decline in 1st/2nd Declension.
Irregular Adjectives

These adjectives are called the irregular adjectives. Your job is to memorize this entire chart because it will be the only way you will recognize them in a Latin sentence. Read each adjective going across from the positive, then the comparative, and to the superlative degree.
