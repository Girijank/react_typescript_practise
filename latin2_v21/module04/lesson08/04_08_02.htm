Incredible Idioms

Every language has expressions whose real meanings are lost when they are translated word for word into another language. Think about the phrase "What's up?" If you did not speak English and were to look up each word in a dictionary, would this phrase have the same meaning as the way we use it today? Latin combines words together to mean one idiomatic phrase. It is important to recognize the idiomatic phrase as a whole so you translate the idiom accurately in a sentence.

The following are idioms found in Latin. In memoria tene (also an idiom) and put them in your Vocabulary Notebook under the heading idioms.
