Mars, God of War

I am Mars, god of war, son of Jupiter and Juno. In all of its battles fought and wars won, the city of Rome should give me all the credit for its mastery in war because I am the one who gave Rome life! Remember that I am the father of Romulus and Remus, so I can take credit for giving Rome its founders. Therefore, I believe Rome owes me credit for all its greatest victories: the Latin Wars, the Etruscan Wars, the Punic Wars, and the wars you’ll learn in this module such as the Jugurthine Wars, the Civil Wars, and the Gallic Wars. Any war that Rome won was definitely because I am Rome’s patron god of war! Sum deus fortis!

In this module, you will discover how Rome continued to grow in size and might, even when challenged by outside opponents or by its own people. Very similar to my own abilities, Rome always persisted, its people remained resolute, and its promise remained indefatigable.

Let’s learn more about how I think and what shaped my own fierce mentality so that you can understand why I see greatness for the future of Rome!
