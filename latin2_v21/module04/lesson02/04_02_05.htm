Superlative Sentence Usage

The superlative adjective can be followed by a word in the Genitive case.

Examples:
  • the most beautiful of all women
  • the fiercest of them all
  • the smallest dog of the litter

Iudex est audacissimus. The judge is the boldest. audacissimus = Nominative sg. m. to agree with iudex.
Romanus consul altissimum ordo in exercitu tenet. The Roman consul holds the highest rank in the army. altissimum = Accusative sg. n. to agree with ordo.
