National Standards

National Standards
Module National Standards
Module One
  • ACL 1.1 Students read, understand, and interpret Latin.
  • ACL 1.2 Students use orally, listen to, and write Latin as part of the language learning process.
  • ACL 2.1 Students demonstrate an understanding of the perspectives of Roman culture as revealed in the practices of the Romans.
  • ACL 2.2 Students demonstrate an understanding of the perspectives of Roman culture as revealed in the products of the Greeks or Romans.
  • ACL 3.1 Students reinforce and further their knowledge of other disciplines through their study of classical languages
  • ACL 3.2 Students expand their knowledge through the reading of Latin and the study of ancient culture.
  • ACL 4.1 Students recognize and use elements of the Latin language to increase knowledge of their own language.
  • ACL 4.2 Students compare and contrast their own culture with that of the Greco-Roman world
