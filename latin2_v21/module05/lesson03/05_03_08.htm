Now, test your knowledge of all the participles we have learned by translating these sentences!

Tres fratres, saxum iacturi, a matre sua castigati sunt.

The three brothers about to throw the rock were punished by their mother.

Augustus, a Romanis amatus, orbem vicit.

Augustus, having been loved by the Romans, conquered the world.

Femina clamans saxum ad portum iecit.

The shouting woman threw a rock at the gate.

Senatores Caesarem, a conspiratoribus necatum, viderunt.

The senators saw Caesar, having been killed by the conspirators.

Imperator, propellens exercitum, hostes vicit.

The emperor, while pushing his army, defeated the enemy.
