Verb Guide
Throughout your Latin studies, you will learn each of these forms.

Tense: The time at which the verb occurs; Latin has six tenses: present, imperfect, future, perfect, pluperfect, and future perfect.

Mood : Classifications that denote the attitude of the speaker; Latin has three moods: indicative, imperative, and subjunctive.

Voice: The relationship between the subject and the verb; Latin has two voices: active and passive.

Conjugation: The inflection of the verb; Latin has four conjugations: 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3rd-io, 4th.
