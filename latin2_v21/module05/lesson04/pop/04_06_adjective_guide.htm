Noun/Adjective/Pronoun Guide
Throughout your Latin studies, you will learn each of these forms.

Declension: The family in which a noun belongs; there are five declensions in Latin

Declension Endings: The group of endings that belong to a declension

Case:The grammatical function the noun/adjective/pronoun performs in the sentence

Declining: The writing of the noun/adjective/pronoun in each of its cases with case endings in singular and plural
