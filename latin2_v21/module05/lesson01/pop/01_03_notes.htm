Verb Guide
Throughout your Latin studies, you will learn each of these forms.
Tense: The time at which the verb occurs. Latin has six tenses: Present, Imperfect, Future, Perfect, Pluperfect, Future Perfect.
Mood: Classifications that denote the attitude of the speaker. Latin has three moods: Indicative, Imperative, Subjunctive.
Voice: The relationship between the subject and the verb. Latin has two voices: active, passive.
Conjugation: The inflection of the verb. Latin has four conjugations: 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3rd-io, 4th.
