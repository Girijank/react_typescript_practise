Navigation—Text Version

Menu

Using the menu on the left allows you to navigate between modules.

Objectives

Why? Why do I have to learn this? The objectives are the “why” behind the “what.” You can find all the objectives on this tab to help you direct your learning and studying. They even update with each page, so that you can be sure exactly what the goal is for each lesson.

Glossary

Using the Glossary button will allow you to review all glossary terms used in the course. You can find all vocabulary terms from your Latin course here, in English to Latin or Latin to English.

ToolBox

What is a toolbox? It’s where you keep the extra tools you need to help you complete the task ahead. Your toolbox has special Latin tools to help you complete this course. We encourage you to reach out to your teacher, but always check the toolbox first when you have questions. You will find extended explanations about the grammar or other concepts that you might need extra help with. Please take some time to explore all the tools at your fingertips.

Resources

The Resources button will open a page with things that will help you in the course, like materials lists, videos, Artist Spotlights, and Art Talk examples.

Speech Tools

To master a language, you should speak it. You will have many opportunities to practice your Latin. If you are unable to access the speech tool, you can find other options here to complete the recordings. Don’t hesitate to reach out to your teacher if you have a concern.

Print

Using the Print Page button allows you to print the lesson page.

Module Progress Bar

Using the module progress bar will help you to see how much of the lesson you have completed already. You can also navigate to other pages within the lesson from the module progress bar.

Home and Arrows

Using the Home icon will be the fastest route back to the course home page. The left arrow navigates to the previous page, and the right arrow navigates to the next page.
