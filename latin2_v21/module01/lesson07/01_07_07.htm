I am Ancus Marcius, 4th King of Rome.

I ruled from: 642 BCE-617 BCE. I am known for: 

  • Being the grandson of Numa Pompilius
  • Establishing the Prison System
  • Defeat of the Latins
  • Addition of Latins to Rome
  • Mamertine Prison

Post Tullum Hostilium, Ancus Marcius, nepos Numae Pompilii, erat rex. Religio a Tullo Hostilio ad cives Romanos rediebatur. Etiam, Ostia, portus Romae, a Anco Marcio aedificabatur.

After Tullus Hostilius, Ancus Marcius, the grandson of Numa Pompilius, was king. Religion was returned to the Roman citizens by Tullus Hostilius. Also, the Ostia, the port of Rome, was built by Ancus Marcius.
