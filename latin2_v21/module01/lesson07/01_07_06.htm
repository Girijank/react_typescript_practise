Juno's Note: Noun and Adjective Agreement

Adjectives agree with nouns in case, number, and gender. Take a look at these examples of how the Case, Number and Gender (CNG) in English and Latin influence the way your noun and adjectives agree!

Examples Noun/Adjective CNG Agreement
He is a good boy. puer bonus—a good boy
  • Nominative (predicate)
  • Singular
  • Masculine
He lives a good life. vitam bonam—good life
  • Accusative (direct object)
  • Singular
  • Feminine

Use these examples of noun-adjective agreement to guide you when you are unsure of what case, gender, and number you should use for a noun-adjective pair!
