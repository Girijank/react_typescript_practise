Tullus Hostilius, 3rd King of Rome

I am Tullus Hostilius, 3rd king of Rome.

I ruled from: 673 BCE-642 BCE. I am known for: 

  • Being a war-like king
  • Building the Curia Hostilia
  • Defeat of the Albans
  • Addition of Albans to Roman Senate

Tullus Hostilius et Ancus Marcius erant tertii et quarti reges Romae. Dum Tullus Hostilius erat rex, viri Albae Longae vincebantur et civitates Romanas dabantur. Etiam, Curia Hostilia a Tullo Hostilio aedificabatur. Religio a Tullo Hostilio relicta est.

Tullus Hostilius and Ancus Marcius were the third and fourth kings of Rome. While Tullus Hostilius was king, the men of Alba Longa were conquered, and they were given Roman citizenship. Also, the Curia Hostilia was built by Tullus Hostilius. Religion was abandoned by Tullus Hostilius.

Vocabulary:

relinquo, relinquere, reliqui, relictus,-a,-um = to leave behind
