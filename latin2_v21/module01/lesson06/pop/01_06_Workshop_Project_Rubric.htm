Latin 2 Workshop Project Rubric (50 points)

Category Proficiency Approaching Proficiency Needs Improvement

Comprehension and Cultural Awareness

20–17 points

The student skillfully completes the following tasks at the appropriate level:

  • understands and responds appropriately to questions
  • asks appropriate questions
  • applies knowledge of culture and customs from Roman history, civilization, and mythology

16–14 points

The student partially completes the following tasks at the appropriate skill level:

  • understands and responds appropriately to questions
  • asks appropriate questions
  • applies knowledge of culture and customs from Roman history, civilization, and mythology

13–0 points

The student is unable to complete the following tasks at the appropriate skill level:

  • understands and responds appropriately to questions
  • asks appropriate questions
  • applies knowledge of culture and customs from Roman history, civilization, and mythology

Grammar and Pronunciation

20–17 points

The student shows a strong command of the Latin language through correct application of grammar concepts to the following translation skills:

  • 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Declension Latin nouns
  • 1st-4th Conjugation verbs in the Active Voice
  • Irregular verbs
  • Infinitives and Imperatives
  • Formation and translation of Passive verbs of the Present System in Latin and English
  • Ablative of Agent

The student demonstrates understanding and application of Classical Latin pronunciation guidelines.

16–14 points

The student shows a limited command of the Latin language through correct application of grammar concepts to the following translation skills:

  • 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Declension Latin nouns
  • 1st-4th Conjugation verbs in the Active Voice
  • Irregular verbs
  • Infinitives and Imperatives
  • Formation and translation of Passive verbs of the Present System in Latin and English
  • Ablative of Agent

The student demonstrates understanding of Classical Latin pronunciation guidelines.

13–0 points

The student shows a weak command of the Latin language through correct application of grammar concepts to the following translation skills:

  • 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Declension Latin nouns
  • 1st-4th Conjugation verbs in the Active Voice
  • Irregular verbs
  • Infinitives and Imperatives
  • Formation and translation of Passive verbs of the Present System in Latin and English
  • Ablative of Agent

The student does not demonstrate understanding of Classical Latin pronunciation guidelines.

Commitment to Learning

10–7 points

The student shows a strong commitment to learning by

  • actively participating in the conversation
  • being prepared to discuss all previous coursework
  • leading discussion through relevant connections to course material where possible

6–4 points

The student shows a limited commitment to learning by

  • partially participating in the conversation
  • being prepared to discuss some previous coursework
  • making relevant connections to course material when prompted

3–0 points

The student shows a lack of commitment to learning by

  • not participating in the conversation
  • not being prepared to discuss previous coursework
  • making no relevant connections to course material
