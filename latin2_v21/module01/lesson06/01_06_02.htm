Listen to the conversation below. Then, choose a side of the conversation to listen to and record yourself repeating to hear and compare your pronunciation. Once you have that side down, repeat the process as the other character!

The more you speak Latin out loud, the more comfortable you will feel pronouncing and discussing the vocabulary and phrases you learn with your instructor. Let's bring this "dead" language to life!
