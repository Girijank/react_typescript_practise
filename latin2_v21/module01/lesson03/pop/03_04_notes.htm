Present Tense Active Voice and the Passive Voice

Present Tense Active Voice

Person Latin Form English Translation
1st person singular (I) amo I love, am loving, do love
2nd person singular (you) amas you love, are loving, do love
3rd person singular (he/she/it) amat he/she/it loves, is loving, does love
1st person plural (we) amamus we love, are loving, do love
2nd person plural (you all) amatis you all love, are loving, do love
3rd person plural (they) amant they love, are loving, do love

Passive Voice

Person Latin Form English Translation
1st person singular (I) amor I am being loved.
2nd person singular (you) amaris You are being loved.
3rd person singular (he/she/it) amatur He, she, it is being loved.
1st person plural (we) amamur We are being loved.
2nd person plural (you all) amamini You all are being loved.
3rd person plural (they) amantur They are being loved.
