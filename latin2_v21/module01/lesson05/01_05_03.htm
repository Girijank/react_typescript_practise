(Example: The city was founded by Dido.)

 Remember:

Use an Ablative of Agent to describe the ‘doers’ (aka ‘by ____’ or ‘a/ab ____’).

In passive sentences, the subject of the verb is being acted upon, rather than doing the action.

 Remember:
