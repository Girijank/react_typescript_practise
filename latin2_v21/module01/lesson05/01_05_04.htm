Latin Writing Assignment

Write three sentences in Latin about Rome’s foundation story. At least two of the three sentences must be written in the Passive Voice.

35–30 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • Content includes three complete sentences in Latin (at least two of which are in the Passive Voice)
  • Content is related to the foundation story of Rome

The sentences:

  • Content use a variety of vocabulary that fits the context of the story
  • Content consistently use correct grammar, syntax, and mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • Content are creative and demonstrate originality

Grammar:

Student uses all of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • Content Present System, Passive Voice verb formation
  • Content Ablative of Agent
  • Content Subject-Verb agreement
  • Content Case endings

29–20 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • Content includes three complete sentences in Latin (at least two of which are in the Passive Voice)
