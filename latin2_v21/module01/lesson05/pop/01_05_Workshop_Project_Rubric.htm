Latin Writing Assignment

Write three sentences in Latin about Rome’s foundation story. At least two of the three sentences must be written in the Passive Voice.

35–30 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • Contentincludes three complete sentences in Latin (at least two of which are in the Passive Voice)
  • Contentis related to the foundation story of Rome

The sentences:

  • Contentuse a variety of vocabulary that fits the context of the story
  • Contentconsistently use correct grammar, syntax, and mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • Contentare creative and demonstrate originality

Grammar:

Student uses all of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • ContentPresent System, Passive Voice verb formation
  • ContentAblative of Agent
  • ContentSubject-Verb agreement
  • ContentCase endings

29–20 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • Contentincludes three complete sentences in Latin (at least two of which are in the Passive Voice)
  • Contentis related to the foundation story of Rome

The sentences:

  • Contentuse a variety of vocabulary that mostly fits the context of the story
  • Contentinclude grammatical structures with errors that generally do not impede comprehension and the mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling) are usually correct
  • Contentare somewhat creative and demonstrate originality

Grammar:

Student uses most of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • ContentPresent System, Passive Voice verb formation
  • ContentAblative of Agent
  • ContentSubject-Verb agreement
  • ContentCase endings

The Latin writing assignment:
