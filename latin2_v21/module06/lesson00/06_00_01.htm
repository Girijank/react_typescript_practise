Poetry and Oration

Just like that, Rome had become a great civilization, spanning a large portion of the European and African continents. With all this growth and new experiences from other cultures encountered along the way, the Romans learned new ways to express themselves. That's where I, Apollo, Olympian deity of song, literature, and all things creative, come in!

Stick with me, and I will introduce you to some of the wonderful ways the Romans sang and spoke of their fabulous homeland. While I am at it, I will introduce myself!
