Poetry and Oration Discussion-Based Assessment

It's time to prepare for your Discussion-Based Assessment—DBA.

Now, it’s time to get ready to discuss poetry and indirect statements with your instructor. Talking about (and in) a foreign language can make even the most veteran speakers nervous sometimes! Use this lesson to practice on your own before your discussion assessment—the more you speak, the more at ease you will feel. Time to dust off this "ancient" language and give it a whirl!

Remember, to help you do well for your DBA, use these practices as much as needed before you contact your teacher.
