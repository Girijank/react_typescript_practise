Ad astra per sententias!

Translate these sentences into English

Pastor sensit oves suas a lupa necatas esse.

The shepherd sensed that his own sheep had been killed by the wolf.

Ille exercitus monstrat tela sua aes et ferrum habere.

That army shows that their own weapons contain copper and iron.

Romani instabunt naves classes hostium superaturas esse.

The Romans will threaten that their ships will defeat the fleet of the enemy.

Piscatores viderunt pisces ex aqua desiluisse.

The fishermen saw that the fish had jumped out of the water.

Apollo sperat puellam pulchram celeriter currentem eum amaturam esse.

Apollo hopes that the beautiful girl running quickly will love him.

Maecenas acer cognoscet poetas suas artes optimas temporum omnium scribere!

The sharp Maecenas will think that his poets are writing the best art of all times!

Tonsor dicens artifici audiebat aves in caelo super forum volare.

The barber speaking to the craftsman was hearing that birds were flying in the sky over the forum.

Novi poetae sribentes in Roma dicunt poemata sua linguam Latinam permutavisse.

The new poets writing in Rome say that their own poems have changed the Latin language.

A Lesbia relictus, Catullus putat se solum futurum esse.

Having been abandoned by Lesbia, Catullus thinks that he himself will be alone.

Bello civili gesso, agricola putavit terras suas a Senatore rapturum iri.

After the civil war had been waged, the farmer thought that his own lands would be seized by the senator.
