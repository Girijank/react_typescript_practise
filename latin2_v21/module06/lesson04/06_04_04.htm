Indirect Statement Sentences

We’re not being indirect…it’s time to practice!

Oportebat hostem oppidum oppugnavisse.

What is the tense of the main verb oportebat? Imperfect
Mark off the full Accusative-infinitive clause in brackets. [hostem oppidum oppugnavisse]
What is the indirect statement infinitive? Oppugnavisse
Name the tense and voice of the indirect statement infinitive. Perfect Active
What is the full sentence translation? It was fitting that the enemy had attacked the town.
