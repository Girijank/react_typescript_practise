Apollo’s Aim: vis, vis

Learn the declension of the noun vis, vis f. force. It is declined differently than other 3rd Declension I-stem nouns.

Case Singular Plural
Nominative vis vires
Genitive vis virium
Dative vi viribus
Accusative vim vires
Ablative vi viribus

Sustinete vi nolite constituere
Nolite constituere dum satis accipiatis!

Keep on with the force don't stop—
