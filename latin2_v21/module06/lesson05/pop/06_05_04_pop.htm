Roman Artist Report

In an English paragraph (400-450 words), write a report based upon the artists you researched.

30–25 points

Roman Artist Report:

  • is 400-450 words
  • consists of full sentences, with correct grammar and punctuation
  • fully demonstrates use of student's own research

Student includes all of the following elements in their narrative:

  • factual and biographical information about the three Roman artists and one modern artist, including artistic works and contributions and descriptive characteristic traits
  • supporting evidence for all of these, linked to student research

  • supporting evidence for all of these, linked to student research

Latin Writing Assignment

Write three sentences in Latin as part of your report. Your sentences must use at least one present tense "sonsory" main verb, one imperfect/perfect tense “sensory” main verb, and one future tense “sensory” main verb..

35–30 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • includes three complete sentences in Latin (At least one contains an indirect statement, using a present tense main “sensory” verb; one contains an indirect statement, using an imperfect/perfect tense main “sensory” verb; and one contains an indirect statement, using a future tense main “sensory” verb.)
  • is related to the written narrative and infused into the report

The sentences:

  • use a variety of vocabulary that fits the context of the story
  • consistently use correct grammar, syntax, and mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • are creative and demonstrate originality

Grammar:

Student uses all of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • indirect statement with a present tense main “sensory” verb with any infinitive tense
  • indirect statement with an imperfect/perfect tense main “sensory” verb with any infinitive tense
  • indirect statement with a future tense main “sensory” verb with any infinitive tense

29–20 points

The Latin writing assignment:

  • is related to the written narrative and infused into the report

The sentences:
