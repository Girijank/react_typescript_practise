A Poet In and Out Of Love

There was a genre in Roman literature called love elegy. Love elegy poets wrote emotional poems that would tear at your heartstrings! The Roman love elegists were:

  • Catullus
  • Gallus
  • Propertius
  • Tibullus
  • Ovid

Catullus, the Love Elegist

Many of Catullus’s poems were addressed to his girlfriend, Lesbia. If you read his poems, you’ll witness a man in love, then out of love, then bitter about his breakup, and then very emotional because he wants Lesbia back. There are so many emotions in his poems that it plays out like a modern-day telenovela!
