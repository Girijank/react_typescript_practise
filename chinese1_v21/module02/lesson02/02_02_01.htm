准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready To Go?

Awesome job accumulating your vocabulary as you move along. Keep the momentum going as we have some more to come! Now imagine you just ordered a cup of tea at the coffee shop, but it’s too hot, or you plan to tell your pen pal in China about your favorite dessert. How do you say these things properly in Chinese? In this lesson, we’ll tackle them all.

After this lesson, you will be able to describe your food and drink, hot or cold, and express your preferences of them— more specifically such as your most or least favorites. Now you will have more to chat about with your pen pal in China. Lots of fun coming your way. Enjoy!
