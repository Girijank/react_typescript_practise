What written work marks the origin of the Chinese tea culture?

“The Classic Art of Tea” by 陆羽 Lù Yǔ (Lu Yu)

What is a characteristic of black tea?

It’s preserved in a special way to keep the tea flavor for long journeys of exporting.

What are the six types of Chinese tea?

Green tea, black tea, oolong tea, white tea, yellow tea, and dark tea

What are the etiquettes to follow when tea is served to you as a guest?

We should receive the tea with both hands, smell the aroma of the tea, and then drink it. We shouldn’t cross legs when drinking tea.
