Notes

  • Adverbs 最 zuì (the most), 太…了 tài…le (too), and 不 bù (not) are used with adjectives or verbs in a sentence.
  • Besides 冷 lěng (cold) and 热 rè (hot), there are other adjectives to describe temperature being hot or cold. You will learn them in the future.
