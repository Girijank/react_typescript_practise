准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

In this lesson, you will continue to expand your horizons on your communication skills involving dining. Think about all the yummy food and drinks you will be able to order at your next visit to a Chinese restaurant.

Have you been wondering how to describe in Chinese some common food items on your dining room table? Do you know how to pay for a meal using the Chinese language? This lesson will help you with just that, and if you get around to going to China, you will be ready to order if you wish to have a bit of “flavor from home.”

In this lesson, you will also meet David Kwok, a middle school student from Hong Kong, and learn about his life and cultural surroundings.

Are you ready? Set and go!
