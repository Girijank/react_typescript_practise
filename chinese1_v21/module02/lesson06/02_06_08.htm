该谁买单 Gāi shéi mǎidān Who's Paying

Ready to leave after a delicious meal at a restaurant? Not yet. You will need to pay first. Do you know how to get the check and pay for a meal in Chinese? You will learn all about it here. Don’t be shy. Just ask for the check.

Note: You may practice writing the characters in the words or phrases in your note guide, but you will not be assessed on writing them.

Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need to sound like a native speaker!


Notes

  • 元 yuán refers to one dollar in any currency. In the future, you will learn how to use 元 yuán to specify different currencies.
  • The phrase 付钱 fùqián (to pay) can be used for paying at any place, while 买单 mǎidān (to pay the bill) is specifically used when you ask to pay the check in a restaurant.
