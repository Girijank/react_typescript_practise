Isn’t it amazing that you are now able to converse about some food items in Chinese and express your preferences on them? Don’t stop here. Let’s explore more.

Consider what your own diet is like at home or in your own culture. Have you noticed any food or drink items that you haven’t learned in this lesson? Also, how is your own diet different from the Chinese diet that’s introduced in this lesson? Here are some missions for you:

  • Is your favorite food taught in this lesson? If not, make a short list of foods you want to know, and ask your instructor how to say them. Now you can say any of your favorite food(s) in Chinese.
  • Compare your own diet with a typical Chinese diet. Pay special attention to the types of foods or drinks you may also see in a typical Chinese diet. Have you found anything interesting? You can share these findings with your instructor.
