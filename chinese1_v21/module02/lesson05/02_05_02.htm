总结 Zǒngjié Recap

Let’s quickly review what you’ve learned in this module so far. Here’s a checklist that you can go through very quickly for a little recap.

  • Say different types of meats, vegetables, and fruits.
  • Say different types of drinks and snacks.
  • Say different types of utensils and courtesy words used in restaurants.
  • Use basic Chinese sentence structure: subject + verb + object.
  • Express likes in Chinese, using the verb 喜欢 xǐhuān.
  • Express degrees of likes, using adverbs of degree or adverbs.
  • Express “most” by using 最 zuì and “not” by using 不 bù.
  • Ask someone’s preferences, using structure: subject + 喜欢 xǐhuān (to like) + object one + 还是 háishì háishì (or) + object two.
  • Ask someone’s likes and dislikes, using 什么 shénme (what).
  • Describe food or drink being hot or cold.
  • Ordering with 我要 Wǒ yào (I want), specifying with measure words and quantities.
  • Inquire what others want, using structure noun/pronoun + 呢 ne (What about… ?).
  • Using “两 Liǎng” and “二 èr”
  • Describe typical diets in the Chinese culture.
  • Explain the Chinese tea culture.
  • Discuss traditional Chinese desserts.
  • Explain table etiquette and rituals in China.
  • Use pinyin components you’ve learned so far (also remember to practice the tones):
    • Initials: c; ch; x
    • Finals: ai, ao, eng, ie

家庭聚餐 Jiātíng jùcān Family Dinner

陈小明 Chén Xiǎomíng and 陈小立 Chén Xiǎolì are cousins. They go to the same middle school, and they sometimes walk home together on Fridays since they usually have family gatherings on weekends. This Friday after school, they meet at the school entrance to walk home together. They are set to chat about the food and drink they will have at the family dinner this coming weekend. 陈小明 Chén Xiǎomíng will start the conversation, and you will take the role of 陈小立 Chén Xiǎolì.

Note: 陈小立 Chén Xiǎolì likes oranges. Her favorite snack is chocolate, and she doesn’t like apple pie. She also thinks that juices are too cold. For the family dinner tomorrow, she plans to have a cup of hot tea and two sandwiches.

Respond to 陈小明 Chén Xiǎomíng.
