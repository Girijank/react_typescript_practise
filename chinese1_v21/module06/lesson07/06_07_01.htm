演讲时间 Yǎnjiǎng shíjiān Presentational Speaking

It is time to showcase your verbal skills on what you have learned. For this presentation, you will record an audio or a video, speaking in complete sentences in Chinese. Your audio or video should include the grammar and vocabulary from the lessons. Using complete sentences in Chinese, talk about the city in China that you’ve researched in the writing workshop previously in this module, for which you have created a simple city map. For this lesson, you will select at least two places on that map that you wish to go, describe their locations relative to the places near them, tell what types of transportation you will use to get there, and say what two activities you can do at each of these two places.

Note

  • To describe the places as your destinations, use the city map you have created for the lesson "My Community Workshop" in this module, and use the structure:

    subject + 要去 yàoqù (be going to) + destination

  • To describe locations of the places, review the “Preposition Words” you have learned in this course, and use the structure:

    Item #1 + 在 zài (in/on/at) + Item #2 + 的 de (of) + preposition word

  • To describe how to get to the places, review the “Types of Transportation” you have learned in this course, and use the structure:

    subject + 坐 zuò/骑 qí + transportation + 去 qù (to go to) + destination

  • To describe activities to do at these places, review the “Pastimes and Activities” you have learned in this course, and use the structure:

    在 zài (in/on/at) + destination + 可以 kěyǐ (may/can) + verb + activity

  2. Know the requirements of your presentation. (Note: The sample English sentences in parentheses are just a guide. You need to come up with your own original sentences in Chinese.)

    • Say what two places you want to go to on the map. (e.g., I am going to the theme park. I am going to the beach.)
    • Say the relative locations of the places. (e.g., The theme park is outside the city district. The beach is near the dock.)
    • Say the types of transportation you will use to get to these places. (e.g., I take the tram to the theme park. I ride a tricycle to the beach.)
    • Say two pastimes or activities you can do at each of the two places. (e.g., At the theme park, you can ride a roller coaster and walk the maze. At the beach, you can surf and build a sandcastle.)
  3. Finalize your oral presentation
    • Your recording should be about 15–30 seconds long.
    • Give your presentation in Chinese, using appropriate vocabulary and grammar.
    • Start your presentation by stating your first and last name in English. (e.g., My name is Angie Liu.)
    • Speak clearly and loudly enough for the microphone to record all your words.
    • Speak at an appropriate rate. (Don’t speak too quickly or too slowly, and do not take extended pauses.)
    • Use correct tones and pronunciations throughout the speech.
    • Practice a couple of times; first in a mirror or in front of a family member or friend.
