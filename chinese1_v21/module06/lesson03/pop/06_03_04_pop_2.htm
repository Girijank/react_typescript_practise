Places to Go in 西单 Xīdān (Xidan)

西单 Xīdān (Xidan) has hundreds of years of history. It originated from Xidan Archway, a small business area, and started booming in the 1900s. It has since developed many stores and businesses. Now 西单 Xīdān (Xidan) is known as the “Shopping Heaven for the Youth.” It has many trendy and modern places young people can enjoy. Below are some places I often go to:

  • 西单文化广场 Xīdān wénhuà guǎngchǎng (Xidan Cultural Square)—a multi-purpose cultural and business square
  • 西单大悦城 Xīdān dàyuè chéng (Xidan Joy City)—an international shopping mall
  • 北京图书大厦 Běijīng túshū dàshà (Beijing Book Building)—one of the largest book markets in Beijing
  • 汉光百货 Hànguāng bǎihuò (Hanguang Department Store)—one of the busiest department stores on Xidan Commercial Street
