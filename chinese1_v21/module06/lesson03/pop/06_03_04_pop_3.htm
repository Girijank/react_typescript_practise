More Places to Go in 西单 Xīdān (Xidan)

Below are some more places to go to in 西单 Xīdān (Xidan):

  • 北京音乐厅 Běijīng yīnyuètīng (Beijing Concert Hall)—one of the first modern concert halls in China
  • 北京民族饭店 Běijīng mínzú fàndiàn (Beijing Minzu Hotel)—a historic four-star business hotel
  • 民族文化宫 Mínzú wénhuàgōng (Culture Palace of Nationalities)—a cultural museum, gallery, and art institute
  • many financial office buildings in 西单 Xīdān (Xidan) such as 中银大厦 Zhōngyín dàshà (BOC Headquarters in Xidan), the headquarter of Bank of China
