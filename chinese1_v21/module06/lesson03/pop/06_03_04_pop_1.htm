City Districts in My City

My city, Beijing, is the capital of the People’s Republic of China. It has sixteen administrative districts, among which are six city districts, such as 东城区 Dōngchéng qū (Dongcheng District), 西城区 Xīchéng qū (Xicheng District), and 海淀区 Hǎidiàn qū (Haidian District).

Each city district has its own downtown area with similar layouts and buildings. I live in 西城区 Xīchéng qū (Xicheng District). The downtown in this district, which is near to my home, is called 西单 Xīdān (Xidan).
