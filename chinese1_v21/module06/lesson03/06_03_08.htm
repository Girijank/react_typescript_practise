Think and Apply

祝贺你！Zhùhè nǐ! Congratulations! You’ve completed this lesson with flying colors! Now, do you think you can showcase what you’ve learned to a family member or friend? You can showcase and teach your family or friend the following:

  • Identify two buildings or businesses you can find in the downtown area of a community, using Chinese.
  • Describe the locations of the two buildings or businesses you just named, using preposition words you learned, in complete sentences in Chinese.
  • Explain how to pronounce initial “b” and final “u,” and compare them to similar pronunciation(s) in your own language.
  • Say a complete sentence, using 吧 ba to give a suggestion to one of your friends or family members.
  • Ask an affirmative-negative question, using 不 bù to one of your friends or family members, and explain to him or her what it is asking, and then expect an answer in English from him or her.
  • Ask a tag question, using 不 bù to one of your friends or family members, and explain to him or her what it is asking, and then expect an answer in English from him or her.
