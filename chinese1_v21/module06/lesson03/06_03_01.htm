准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Job well done learning all about your and your friend’s pastimes. Excellent! Now, what about spending some time in the city? Imagine you have friends visiting from China who wish to check out the city you are in. The best place you can show off your city would be the downtown area. In this lesson, we will explore the vocabulary related to places you can take your friend to and how you can describe their locations with accuracy in Chinese. The fun is ahead and let’s go in town!


After this lesson, you will be able to show your friends from China your own city in Chinese and with pride. Way to go!
