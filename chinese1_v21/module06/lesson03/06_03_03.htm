Notes

The last two characters, 中心 zhōngxīn, in the word 市中心 shìzhōngxīn (downtown) mean “center.” It can be used with other words to form new phrases. For example, 购物中心 gòuwùzhōngxīn (shopping center) and 活动中心 huódòngzhōngxīn (activity center).
