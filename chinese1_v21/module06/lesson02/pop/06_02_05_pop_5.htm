Going Shopping in My Community

  • People in my community enjoy shopping a lot. There are many places in my community where we can shop for all kinds of things. My friend and I like to go to the big shopping mall that has department stores.
  • My mom and her friends like to go shopping at small shops in the back streets where they often find good bargains.
  • My dad and his friends like to go to flea markets and shop for antiques. They sometimes find ancient treasures there. They also like to go to the flower and bird market for seasonal discounts.
