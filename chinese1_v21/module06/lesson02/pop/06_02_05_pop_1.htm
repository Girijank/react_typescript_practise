Senior Pastime in My Community

  • Seniors in my community enjoy outdoor activities that are mostly moderate and relaxing, such as tai chi, which sometimes involves the use of a Chinese sword with dull blades for safety.
  • Seniors in my community like to play shuttlecock, a traditional toy usually made with poultry feathers fixed on top of a coin. The player kicks the toy in the air with different tricks and keeps it from falling to the ground.
