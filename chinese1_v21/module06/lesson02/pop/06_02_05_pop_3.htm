Popular Activities in My Community

  • Mahjong is a popular table game in China. It requires four people and uses strategies and patience.
  • People of all ages in my community enjoy karaoke. We sing karaoke at birthday parties or other celebrations.
  • Town square dance is also very popular. In summer evenings after dinner, people will gather outside of the apartment buildings, play popular music, and dance together. It always attracts people walking by or even tourists who join in.
  • Young people also show their own styles through street dancing. Break dancing is very popular.
