Notes

  • The word 散步 sànbù (to walk) means to walk as an activity. For walking to somewhere as a means of moving, there’s a verb for it: 走 zǒu (to walk).
  • The verb 练习 liànxí (to exercise/to practice) can be used for 瑜伽 yújiā (yoga). The character 习 xí can be omitted, and you can say 练瑜伽 Liàn yújiā (to practice yoga). In addition, 瑜伽 yújiā (yoga) is a borrowed word from English.
    Review and select from a list of the borrowed words you've learned and will learn later in this course.
  • Both verbs 看 kàn (to watch) and 听 tīng (to listen to) can be used for 演唱会 yǎnchànghuì (concert). You can say either 看演唱会 kàn yǎnchànghuì or 听演唱会 tīng yǎnhànghuì to indicate attending a concert.
