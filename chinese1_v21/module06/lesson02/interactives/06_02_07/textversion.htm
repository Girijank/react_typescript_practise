What are the four types of musical instruments in China?


Woodwind such as dizi, percussion such as gong, bowed strings such as erhu, and plucked and struck strings such as pipa

What is the most popular and dominant form of Chinese opera?


Peking Opera, also known as Beijing Opera, that combines music, vocal performance, acrobat, and dance

What is pingtan and where does it originate from?


Pingtan is an artistic and musical performance with local dialect. It originates in Suzhou in Southern China. It’s performed by two people accompanied by instruments such as a pipa, and the performance usually tells a story.

Who is considered the forefather of Chinese rock?


Cui Jian, an '80s rock star, is considered the forefather of Chinese rock.
