准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

You’ve just learned to describe different places or businesses in a community. How wonderful! You’ve also learned how to describe places you’ve been to using Chinese, and you’ve learned about popular places in Chinese communities. Now let’s focus on pastime activities you can do at these places. Imagine you are talking with your friend about your weekend plans. How do you tell your friend that you are going shopping or to a concert, and how do you describe some exercises you will do, such as jogging or swimming? We’ll learn all of this. Now you can touch on more topics when conversing with a friend from China, and you can share more with each other!
