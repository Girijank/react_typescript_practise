What are some pastime activities among seniors in Lili’s and Jack’s communities, respectively?

Seniors in Lili’s community usually do tai chi, play shuttlecock, and keep birds as a hobby. Seniors in Jack’s community play bingo and join exercise clubs, jewelry-making clubs, or book clubs.

What are some popular activities among all people in Lili’s and Jack’s communities, respectively?

Popular activities in Lili’s community include mahjong, karaoke, town square dancing, and street dancing for young people. Popular activities in Jack’s community include hiking, camping, and skateboarding for young people.

What are some common shopping places people go to in Lili’s and Jack’s communities, respectively?

People in Lili’s community shop at shopping malls and department stores, small shops in the back streets, flea markets, and flower and bird markets. People in Jack’s community shop at shopping malls or downtown stores, flea markets, farmers markets, and pet stores.
