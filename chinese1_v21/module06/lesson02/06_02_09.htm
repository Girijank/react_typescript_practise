问问题 Wèn wèntí Ask A Question

The W’s

When describing an activity with information such as where, when, who, or with whom in a Chinese sentence, you need to go in the order of “Who + with whom + when + where + doing what.” Remember the “when” doesn’t go at the end like in English. You will need this information to complete your voice activity later in this lesson.
Select each part of the example sentence to see the order in action!

Example :

我和我妹妹下午三点在公园散步。Wǒ hé wǒ mèimei xiàwǔ sāndiǎn zài gōngyuán sànbù. (My younger sister and I walk at the park at 3 p.m.)

他和他朋友星期六上午在活动中心打乒乓球。Tā hé tā péngyǒu xīngqīliù shàngwǔ zài huódòngzhōngxīn dǎ pīngpāngqiú. (He and his friends play ping-pong at the activity center on Saturday morning.)


Do you wonder how to ask someone what pastime they like? It’s actually very simple, so let’s get to it now.


A question with 吗 ma

Do you know that you can change a sentence into a question simply by adding 吗 ma? To do that, you simply add it at the end of a sentence, and change the period to a question mark.

For example:

  • 今天很热。JĪntiān hěn rè.
    It’s very hot today.
  • 今天很热吗？JĪntiān hěn rè ma?
    Is it very hot today?
A tag question with 吗 ma

A tag question is a question that is converted from a statement, asking for confirmation. For example, “It’s hot today, isn’t it?”

Use the following structure to form a tag question in Chinese:

Statement, 好hǎo/是shì/可以kěyǐ + 吗？

For example:

  • 今天很热。JĪntiān hěn rè.
    It’s very hot today.
  • 今天很热，是吗？Jīntiān hěn rè, shì ma?
    It is very hot today, isn’t it?
When to use which

You will use one of the four words 好 hǎo/是 shì/可 以 kěyǐ with 吗 ma, depending on the nature of your tag question.

  • 好吗？hǎo ma? – “okay?”
    For example :
    我们去散步，好吗？Wǒmen qù sànbù, hǎo ma?
    Let’s go for a walk, okay?

  • 是吗？shì ma? – “isn’t it?” (or similar tag questions)
    For example :
    她喜欢独处，是吗？Tā xǐhuān dúchǔ, shì ma?
    She likes to spend time alone, doesn’t she?

  • 可以吗？kěyǐ ma? – “may I” (ask for permission)
    For example :
    妈妈，我想吃蛋糕，可以吗？Māma, wǒ xiàng chī dàngāo, kěyǐ ma?
    Mom, I want to eat cake, may I?
