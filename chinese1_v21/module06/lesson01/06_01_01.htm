准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Excellent job getting your chores done in your home! Now let’s step out and join the community activities. You will learn how to describe different facilities or buildings in your communities and things you can do around them.

Imagine you have a friend from China visiting you who is curious about what your life is like in your community. How do you describe the various local markets, shops or stores, and places you usually go for activities or recreation, using Chinese? How will you tell your friends what facilities your community has or does not have? In this lesson, we’ll learn all about it.


After this lesson, you will be able to give your friend a grand tour around town, using Chinese. Totally wonderful!
