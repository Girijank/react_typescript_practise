Notes

  • In the word 店铺 diànpù (store), the first character, 店 diàn, can be used by itself for the same meaning, “store.” It can be combined with other words to form new phrases. For example, it can be 书店 shūdiàn (bookstore).
  • In the word 购物中心 gòuwùzhōngxīn (shopping center), the last two characters, 中心 zhōngxīn, mean “center.” It can be combined with other words to form new phrases. For example, it can be 市中心 shì zhōngxīn (city center/downtown).

It's Also Said

  • The word 面包店 miànbāodiàn (bakery) is also said as 蛋糕店 dàngāodiàn in some Chinese-speaking regions.
  • The word “store” is sometimes translated as “商店 shāngdiàn.”
  • The word “超市 chāoshì” sometimes also refers to “supermarket” in some Chinese-speaking regions, especially in big cities.
