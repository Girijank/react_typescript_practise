Notes

  • In the word 电影院 diànyǐngyuàn (movie theater), the first two characters, 电影 diànyǐng, mean “movie.” It can be combined with other words to form new phrases. For example, you can say 看电影 kàn diànyǐng (to watch a movie).
  • In the word 理发店 lǐfàdiàn (barbershop), the first two characters, 理发 lǐfà, mean “to get a haircut."

It’s Also Said

The word 药店 yàodiàn (pharmacy) is also said as 药坊 yàofāng or 药局 yàojú in some Chinese-speaking regions.
