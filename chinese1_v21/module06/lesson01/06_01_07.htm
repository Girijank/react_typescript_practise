Notes

  • In the word 活动中心 huódòngzhōngxīn (activity center), the first two characters, 活动 huódòng, mean “activity/event.”
  • In the word 健身房 jiànshēnfáng (gym), the first two characters, 健身 jiànshēn, mean “to work out.”
  • In the word 游泳池 yóuyǒngchí (swimming pool), the first two characters, 游泳 yóuyǒng, mean “to go swimming.”
