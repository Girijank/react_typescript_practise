我的新社区 Wǒde xīn shèqū My New Community


敏仪 Mǐnyí and 婷婷 Tíngting are close friends. They used to be neighbors. Earlier this year, 婷婷 Tíngting’s family moved to a new city. 婷婷 Tíngting recently started exploring her new communitiy and the downtown area of her new city. During summer break, 敏仪 Mǐnyí gets to visit 婷婷 Tíngting for a whole weekend. It’s now Saturday morning, and the two discuss 婷婷 Tíngting’s new community and what activities they can do around town. 敏仪 Mǐnyí starts the conversation, and you will take the role of 婷婷 Tíngting to answer 敏仪 Mǐnyí’s questions, based on the information given below.

婷婷 Tíngting’s new community has a bakery and a grocery store. It also has a movie theater and an activity center. 婷婷 Tíngting hasn’t been to her new community’s movie theater, but she’s been to the activity center. There is a gym there where she gets to work out and practice yoga. Besides these activities, 婷婷 Tíngting also likes to go to the park for a walk and take pictures. In 婷婷 Tíngting’s downtown there’s a library, and 婷婷 Tíngting would like for them to go there to read books that afternoon.

Chinese Characters

敏仪: 你们社区有什么店铺和房子？

婷婷: 我们社区有面包店和超市，也有电影院和活动中心。

敏仪: 你去过你们社区的电影院吗？

婷婷: 我没有去过。我去过我们社区的活动中心。

敏仪: 你为什么去活动中心？

婷婷: 因为活动中心有健身房，我可以健身和练瑜伽。

敏仪: 你还喜欢什么休闲活动？

婷婷: 我喜欢在公园散步和拍照。

敏仪: 你们市中心有图书馆吗？

婷婷: 有。图书馆在剧院的旁边。

敏仪: 今天下午我们做什么？？

婷婷: 我们去图书馆看书吧！

Pinyin

Mǐnyí: Nǐmen shèqū yoǔ shénme diànpù hé fángzi?

Tíngting: Wǒmen shèqū yǒu miànbāodiàn hé chāoshì, yě yǒu diànyǐngyuàn hé huódòngzhōngxīn.

Mǐnyí: Nǐ qùguò nǐmen shèqū de diànyǐngyuàn ma?

Tíngting: Wǒ méiyǒu qùguò. Wǒ qùguò wǒmen shèqū de huódòngzhōngxīn.

Mǐnyí: Nǐ wèishénme qù huódòngzhōngxīn?

Tíngting: Yīnwèi huódòngzhōngxīn yǒu jiànshēnfáng, wǒkěyǐ jiànshēn hé liàn yújiā.

Mǐnyí: Nǐ hái xǐhuān shénme xiūxiánhuódòng?

Tíngting: Wǒ xǐhuān zài gōngyuán sànbù hé pāizhào.

Mǐnyí: Nǐmen shìzhōngxīn yǒu túshūguǎn ma?

Tíngting: Yǒu. Túshūguǎn zài jùyuàn de pángbiān.

Mǐnyí: Jintiān xiàwǔ wǒmen zuò shénme?

Tíngting: Wǒmen qù túshūguǎn kànshū ba.

English

Minyi: What stores or buildings are in your community?

Tingting: Our community has a bakery and a grocery store; it also has a movie theater and an activity center.

Minyi: Have you been to your community’s movie theater?

Tingting: I haven’t been to the movie theater. I’ve been to my community’s activity center.

Minyi: Why did you go to the activity center?

Tingting: Because there is a gym at the activity center, and I can work out and practice yoga.

Minyi: What other pastimes do you like?

Tingting: I like to walk and take pictures at the park.

Minyi: Does your downtown have library?

Tingting: Yes there is. The library is next to the theater.

Minyi: What do we do this afternoon?

Tingting: Let’s go read books at the library!
