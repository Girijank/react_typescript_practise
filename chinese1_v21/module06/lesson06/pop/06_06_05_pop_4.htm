The Modern Times

  • Many modern technologies are now used in the transportation system in my city.
  • Last year when we took the Gaotie, we went through the check-in in seconds with a facial recognition system. The system can even check our temperatures. I heard this system will be used on buses very soon.
  • We can now purchase airplane or train tickets on the phone, using apps such as AliPay or WeChat. They also have services such as refunds, exchanges, and AI-based information centers.
