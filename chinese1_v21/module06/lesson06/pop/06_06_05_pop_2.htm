How We Get to Places

  • My parents and I usually take buses to work or school. My mom’s work is far, and she needs to take two bus routes to get there.
  • My cousin, who just graduated from college and started working, rides the subway to work every day. She says it’s always crowded on the subway.
  • My family visits my relatives in another city, Shanghai, every summer. We usually get there by taking an airplane, or a high-speed train called a Gaotie.
