Chinese Characters

陈丽丽：我今天想去图书馆看书，你去不去？

张明：我今天不想看书。我们一起去市中心，好不好？

陈丽丽：市中心有什么好玩的？

张明：你看，市中心有广场，有博物馆，还有电影院和剧院。我们可以在那里玩一整天。

陈丽丽：我要想一下。

张明：你再看，剧院旁边是警察局和消防站，消防站后边是医院。你很安全的。不要想了，走吧！

陈丽丽：就听你的吧。我们怎么去市中心？

张明：我们先走路去巴士站，坐一号巴士去体育场站，在体育场站坐十二号巴士去市中心。

陈丽丽：好麻烦啊！我们坐出租车去吧！

张明：我今天没有带很多钱。要不我们骑单车去？

陈丽丽：要骑单车还不如去乡村呢！

张明：是吗？乡村有什么好玩的？

陈丽丽：乡村有湖和山。我们可以拍照和散步，还可以在旁边的农场买新鲜水果和蔬菜。

张明：听起来很有趣。我们怎么去乡村？

陈丽丽：我们骑单车从这条路一直走，看到清真寺就右转，再骑三十分钟就到了。

张明：好的，二话不说，我们走吧！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Wǒ jīntiān xiàng qù túshūguǎn kàn shū, nǐ qùbúqù?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ jīntiān bùxiǎng kànshū. Wǒmen yīqǐ qù shìzhōngxīn, hǎobùhǎo?

Chén Lìli: Shì zhōngxīn yǒu shénme hǎowán de?

Zhāng Míng: Nǐkàn, shìzhōngxīn yǒu guǎngchǎng, yǒu bówùguǎn, háiyǒu diànyǐngyuàn hé jùyuàn. Wǒmen kěyǐ zài nàlǐ wán yī zhěng tiān.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ yào xiǎngyīxià.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ zài kàn, jùyuàn pángbiān shì jǐngchájú hé xiāofángzhàn. Xiāofángzhàn hòubiān shì yīyuàn. Nǐ hěn ānquán de. Bùyào xiǎng le, zǒuba!

Chén Lìli: Jiù tīng nǐde ba. Wǒmen zěnme qù shìzhōngxīn?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒmen xiān zǒulù qù bāshìzhàn, zuò yīhào bāshì qù tǐyùchǎng zhàn, zài tǐyùchǎng zhàn zuò shíèrhào bāshì qù shìzhōngxīn.

Chén Lìli: Hǎo máfán a! Wǒmen zuò chūzūchē qù ba!

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ jīntiān méiyǒu dài hěnduō qián. Yàobù wǒmen qí dānchē qù?

Chén Lìli: Yào qídānchē háibùrú qù xiāngcūn ne!

Zhāng Míng: Shìma? Xiāngcūn yǒu shénme hǎowán de?

Chén Lìli: Xiāngcūn yǒu hú hé shān. Wǒmen kěyǐ pāizhào hé sànbù, hái kěyǐ zài pángbiān de nóngchǎng mǎi xīnxiān shuǐguǒ hé shūcài.

Zhāng Míng: Tīng qǐlái hěn yǒuqù. Wǒmen zěnme qù xiāngcūn?

Chén Lìli: Wǒmen qí dānchē cóng zhètiáo lù yīzhí zǒu, kàn dào qīngzhēnsì jiù yòuzhuǎn, zài qí sānshí fēnzhōng jiù dào le.

Zhāng Míng: Hǎode, èrhuàbùshuō, wǒmen zǒuba!

English

Chen Lili: I want to go to the library to read today; will you go?

Zhang Ming: I don’t want to read today. How about we go downtown together?

Chen Lili: What is so fun downtown?

Zhang Ming: Look, downtown there is a plaza, there’s a museum, there is a movie theater, and a theatre. We can spend the whole day there.

Chen Lili: I have to think about it.

Zhang Ming: Look again, there is a police station and fire station next to the theater, and there is a hospital behind the fire station. You are very safe. No more thinking— and let’s go!

Chen Lili: Then I’ll listen to you. How do we get downtown?

Zhang Ming: We will walk to the bus station first, then ride bus #1 to the stadium station. Then, at the stadium station, we will ride bus #12 to downtown.

Chen Lili: It’s so much trouble! Let’s take a taxi!

Zhang Ming: I didn’t bring a lot of money. How about we ride bikes to go there?

Chen Lili: If we ride bikes, we might as well go to the countryside!

Zhang Ming: Is that so? What is so fun in the countryside?

Chen Lili: There are mountains and lakes in the countryside. We can take pictures, go on walks, and we can buy fresh fruit and vegetables at the nearby farm.

Zhang Ming: Sounds very interesting. How do we get to the countryside?

Chen Lili: We ride bikes along this road, and when we see the mosque, we turn right. Then we ride another 30 minutes, and then we are there.

Zhang Ming: Okay. Say no more, and let’s go!
