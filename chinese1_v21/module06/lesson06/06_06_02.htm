声母韵母 Shēngmǔ yùnmǔ Initials and Finals

In this lesson, we will learn the initial “r” and the final “er.”

Initial “r”

This initial sounds like “r” as in “red” in English. But you will need to curl the tongue upwards to make the sound. It’s used in words such as “日 rì (day)”” and “肉 ròu (meat).”

Final “er”

This final sounds like “er” as in “teacher” in English. It is normally used by itself without an initial. It’s used in words such as “儿子 érzi (son)“” and “二 èr (two).”
