Navigation: Know Your Way Around

Just like every pig is unique (no really, they are!), so is every course! Complete the following interactive to see what is new or unique for this course's navigation.

Home Page

This is the first page you see when logging on the course.

The horizontal bar under the course title includes two buttons on both ends: Getting Started and Collaboration. The Getting Started button is what led you to this document. The Collaboration button leads you to the details of the collaboration opportunities you have in both segments of this course.

The round numerical buttons under the horizontal bar will lead you to modules 1 My Life to 6 My Community, respectively. There are six modules in this course. Remember that Segment 1 includes modules 1 My Life, 2 My Preferences, 3 My Family, and Segment 2 includes modules 4 My School, 5 My House, 6 My Community.

When you select each module button, a list of the lessons in that module will pull up. You can then select each of them to get to a lesson.

Lesson Page

At the top of each page in the course, you will find a navigation bar that contains a series of buttons. On the left, you will see the Menu, Glossary, Toolbox, Resources, Speech Tools, and Print buttons. To the right, you will see a Home button with arrows.
