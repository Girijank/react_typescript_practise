Welcome to Chinese 1

You are about to start an amazing journey in which you will explore and learn one of the most ancient and widely used languages in the world, as well as the culture behind it. In this course, you will gradually accumulate vocabulary and grammar knowledge and build your interpretive and communication skills in the Chinese language. Also, be ready to connect your own experience on language and culture with the learning through various communications and comparisons.

Formal Chinese

The official verbal format of Chinese language used in China is called Mandarin (also known as “Standard Chinese”). There are many Chinese dialects, such as Cantonese, Shanghainese, and Hokkienese. In this course, you are learning Mandarin Chinese, the official verbal format of the language.

Chinese Characters

The essence of written Chinese language is Chinese characters, which are always square-shaped. There are two forms of Chinese characters currently in use: simplified Chinese characters and traditional Chinese characters. In this course, you are learning simplified Chinese characters.

Hanyu Pinyin

Hanyu pinyin, commonly known as pinyin, is a phonetic tool you will use to know how to pronounce Chinese characters. There are other similar phonetic tools, such as bopomofo. In this course, you are learning pinyin as the phonetic tool you need to learn the Chinese characters. Throughout the course, you will be provided with pinyin for the Chinese characters you are learning to help you pronounce them.

Closed Captioning/Text Versions

Dialogue videos presented in this course all include closed captions that are embedded in the player. The closed captions include Chinese characters, pinyin, and English. Note that the English is not the exact translation of the Chinese. It gives the overall meaning to help you understand the Chinese. This is your opportunity to show what you know without any hints.

Throughout the course, text versions are provided for any interactives. They provide the same content in text-only format.
