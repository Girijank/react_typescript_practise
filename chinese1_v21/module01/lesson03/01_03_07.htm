发如雪 Fà rú xuě Snowy Hair

Have you heard of a Chinese song called 发如雪 (Fà rú xuě)? It’s a popular song by contemporary Taiwan singer 周杰伦 Zhōu Jiélún (Jay Chou), who has written quite a few masterpieces for the Chinese music world. This song title means “hair like snow,” which relates to our new set of vocabulary to describe one’s hair. We are one more step further toward Chinese pop culture and enriched conversations in Chinese.
