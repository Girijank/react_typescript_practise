准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Job well done so far—learning how to introduce basic information such as your name, origin, and phone number. In this lesson, we’ll learn how to ask and describe you or your friend’s appearance or personality. Sit tight for another exciting ride!

In this lesson, you will continue to make additions to your repository of Chinese pinyin initials and finals, along with continued practice on tones; then you will explore how to ask for and discuss one’s appearance and personality. You will be equipped with new sentence structures, adjectives on colors, as well as adverbs of degree such as “very” and “a little.” Enjoy!
