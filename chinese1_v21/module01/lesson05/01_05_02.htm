总结 Zǒngjiē Recap

Let’s quickly review what you’ve learned in this module so far. Here’s a checklist that you can go through for a little recap.

  • Hi and bye in Chinese
  • Greetings for different times of the day
  • Ask and tell one’s full name or given name
  • Ask and tell one’s surname
  • “Very nice to meet you”
  • Ask and tell area (e.g. state or city) where one lives
  • Ask and tell one’s origin
  • Pronouns: “you,” “I,” “he,” “she,” and “it”
  • Numbers 0 to 10
  • Two-digit numbers
  • Ask and tell one’s phone number
  • Ask and tell one’s physical or personal features
  • Describe eye colors and hair colors
  • Pinyin components you’ve learned so far (also remember to practice the tones):
    • Initials: h; sh
    • Finals: o, ua, i,e

Chén Xiǎolì: Wǒ yě shì.
