Did somebody mention phone numbers? Now let’s see how phone numbers are said in Chinese. In fact, it’s as simple as one-two-three. That’s right. To say phone numbers in Chinese, we simply say them digit by digit with nothing else added, and that’s it.

Notes

When asking phone numbers in Chinese, sometimes we can omit the word “号码 hàomǎ number” (e.g., when we are in a hurry), so the question can simply be: 你的电话是多少？Nǐde diànhuà shì duōshǎo? What’s your phone (number)?
