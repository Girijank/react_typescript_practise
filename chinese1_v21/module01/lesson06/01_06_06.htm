关于职业 Guānyú zhíyè About Professions

In Chinese society, people can be addressed either by their titles (several of which we have learned) or by their professions. For example, they can be addressed as Teacher Wu, Doctor Liu, Professor Li, and so on. However, note that in the Chinese language, a title or profession is placed after one’s name, not before.

Let’s review some vocabulary on professions; then on the next page, we’ll discuss in detail how to properly address someone in Chinese society.
