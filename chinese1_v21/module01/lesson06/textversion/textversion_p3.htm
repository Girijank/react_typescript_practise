Chinese Characters

张明: 这是谁？

陈丽丽:这是我的朋友 Cathy。我们要去她的家。

张明: Cathy 住在哪里？

陈丽丽: 她住在 Tanner Avenue.

张明: 她姓什么？

陈丽丽: 她姓 Smith.

张明: Cathy 是什么样的人？

陈丽丽: 她很开朗。

张明: 她长什么样？

陈丽丽: 她比较高。她的头发是金色的，眼睛是蓝色的。

张明: 我会很高兴认识她，不过我有一点害羞。

陈丽丽: 别担心。她的爸爸 Smith 先生和妈妈 Smith 太太都很好。Smith 先生是一个医生。

张明: Smith 先生今年多大？

陈丽丽: 他五十岁。

陈丽丽:我妈妈来了。走吧！

张明:好的。

Pinyin

Zhāng Míng: Zhè shì shéi?

Chén Lìli: Zhè shì wǒde pěngyǒu Cathy. Wǒmen yào qù táde jiā.

Zhāng Míng: Cathy zhùzài nǎlǐ?

Chén Lìli: Tā zhùzài Tanner Avenue.

Zhāng Míng: Tā xìng shénme?

Chén Lìli: Tā xìng Smith.

Zhāng Míng: Cathy shì shénme yàng de rén?

Chén Lìli: Tā hěn kāilǎng.

Zhāng Míng: Tā zhǎng shénme yàng?

Chén Lìli: Tā bǐjiào gāo. Tāde tóufà shì jīnsè de, yǎnjīng shì lánsè de.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ huì hěngāoxìng rènshi tā, búguò wǒ yǒuyīdiǎn hàixiū.

Chén Lìli: Bié dānxīn. Tāde bàba Smith xiānsheng hé māma Smith tàitai dōu hěnhǎo. Smith xiānsheng shì yīgè yīshēng.

Zhāng Míng: Smith xiānsheng jīnnián duōdà?

Chén Lìli: Tā wǔshí suì.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ māma lái le. Zǒu ba!

Zhāng Míng: Hǎo de

English

Zhang Ming: Who is this?

Chen Lili: This is my friend Cathy. We are going to her house.

Zhang Ming: Where does Cathy live?

Chen Lili: She lives on Tanner Avenue.

Zhang Ming: What’s her last name?

Chen Lili: Her last name is Smith.

Zhang Ming: What is Cathy like?

Chen Lili: She’s very outgoing.

Zhang Ming: What does she look like?

Chen Lili: She’s somewhat tall. Her hair is blonde. Her eyes are blue.

Zhang Ming: I will be glad to meet her, but I am a little shy.

Chen Lili: No worries. Her dad, Mr. Smith, and mom, Mrs. Smith, are both very nice. Mr. Smith is a doctor.

Zhang Ming: How old is Mr. Smith this year?

Chen Lili: He is fifty years old.

Chen Lili: My mom is here. Let’s go!

Zhang Ming: Okay.
