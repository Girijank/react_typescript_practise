准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

In this lesson, you will continue to explore your communication skills. You are on track to becoming a fluent Chinese speaker. How awesome!

When meeting a friend from China, whether in person or online, do you know how to address them properly in Chinese and how to ask for their ages? Well, you are about to address those topics now!

You will also meet Karen Lu, a Chinese American girl from New York, and learn about her life and cultural surroundings. So, no need to take a number, this lesson is calling your name.
