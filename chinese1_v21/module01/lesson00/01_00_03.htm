International Chinese – A Quick Guide to China

The Chinese language is widely spoken worldwide in regions including China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and Chinese expatriate regions, such as Singapore. Later in this course we’ll explore how Chinese is spoken or used in these regions, but let’s take a quick look at China.

China

  • The People’s Republic of China is located in eastern Asia. It’s a vast country with extraordinary geographic diversity and has a population of 1.4 billion people.
  • The official Chinese used in China is called Mandarin Chinese (also known as “Standard Chinese”), which is also what we will learn in this course.
  • There are many dialects in China such as Cantonese, Shanghainese, Hakkanese, and Hokkienese. However, Mandarin is taught in all areas of the country.
