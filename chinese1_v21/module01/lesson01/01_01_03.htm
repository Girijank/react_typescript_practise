你好朋友 Nǐhǎo péngyǒu Hello Friend

How do we greet friends in Chinese? How do we address them? Let’s grab a few words to get started. This will also give you a sense of how Chinese words are pronounced.

You will practice and learn your vocabulary at the same time. Simply click on each image below to bring out your “learn and practice area.” After this practice, you will be ready to speak your first Chinese words!

Note: In this course, we will concentrate mostly on listening, speaking, and some reading skills. Since writing Chinese characters is a bit more complex, we will focus on a few characters per module. In each lesson, these characters are in bold type and highlighted in dark red and you will learn how to write them later in the module. For characters not in bold type or highlighted, feel free to practice writing them in your note guide, but remember that for now, you will only need to know how to write the bold type or highlighted characters.


Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need to sound like a native speaker!
