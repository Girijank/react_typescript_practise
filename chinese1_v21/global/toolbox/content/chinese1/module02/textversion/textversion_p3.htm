Chinese Characters

陈丽丽：张明，你想吃什么？

张明：我们看看菜单。

张明：你喜欢面条还是米饭？

陈丽丽：我喜欢吃米饭。你喜欢吃牛肉还是鸡肉？

张明：我最喜欢鱼肉。你喜欢什么蔬菜?

陈丽丽: 我比较喜欢西兰花。你呢？

张明：我不喜欢胡萝卜。我有点喜欢白菜。

陈丽丽：我也喜欢白菜。

张明: 你喜欢喝果汁吗？

陈丽丽：不喜欢。果汁有点冷。我喜欢喝热茶。你呢？

张明: 我喜欢喝牛奶。热茶太热了。

陈丽丽：我不喜欢喝牛奶。我喜欢喝苏打。

张明: 你喜欢三明治吗？

陈丽丽：我很喜欢吃三明治。我也喜欢曲奇和苹果派。

张明：我喜欢巧克力。太好吃了！

陈丽丽：我也是！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Zhāng Míng, nǐ xiǎng chī shénme?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒmen kànkàn càidān.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ xǐhuān miàntiáo háishì mǐfàn?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ xǐhuān chī mǐfàn. Nǐ xǐhuān chī niúròu háishì jīròu?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ zuì xǐhuān yúròu. Nǐ xǐhuān shénme shūcài?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ bǐjiào xǐhuān xīlánhuā. Nǐne?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ bù xǐhuān húluóbo. Wǒ yǒudiǎn xǐhuān báicài.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ yě xǐhuān báicài.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ xǐhuān hē guǒzhī ma?

Chén Lìli: Bù xǐhuān. Guǒzhī yǒudiǎn lěng. Wǒ xǐhuān hē rè chá. Nǐne?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ xǐhuān hē niúnǎi. Rè chá tài rè le

Chén Lìli: Wǒ bù xǐhuān hē niúnǎi. Wǒ xǐhuān hē sūdǎ.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ xǐhuān sānmíngzhì ma?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ hěn xǐhuān chī sānmíngzhì. Wǒ yě xǐhuān qǔqí hé píngguǒ pài.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ xǐhuān qiǎokèlì. Tài hǎochī le!

Chén Lìli: Wǒyěshì!

English

Chen Lili: Zhang Ming, what do you want to eat?

Zhang Ming: Let’s look at the menu.

Zhang Ming: Do you like noodles or rice?

Chen Lili: I like to eat rice. Do you like to eat beef or chicken?

Zhang Ming: I like fish the most. What vegetables do you like?

Chen Lili: I somewhat like broccoli. What about you?

Zhang Ming: I don’t like carrots, but I like cabbage a little.

Chen Lili: I also like cabbage.

Zhang Ming: Do you like to drink juice?

Chen Lili: I don’t. Juices are a little cold. I like to drink hot tea. What about you?

Zhang Ming: I like to drink milk. Hot tea is too hot.

Chen Lili: I don’t like to drink milk. I like to drink soda.

Zhang Ming: Do you like sandwiches?

Chen Lili: I like to eat sandwiches very much. I also like cookies and apple pie.

Zhang Ming: I like chocolate. Too delicious!

Chen Lili: Likewise!
