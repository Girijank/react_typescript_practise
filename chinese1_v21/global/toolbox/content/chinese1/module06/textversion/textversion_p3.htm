Chinese Characters

张明：我们今天去哪里？

陈丽丽：你看，这是我们社区的地图。

张明：你们社区很漂亮！你们社区有很多店铺和楼房吗？

陈丽丽：是的，有很多。你看，这里是超市和电影院。电影院左边是活动中心和社区游泳池，游泳池左边是小公园，公园左边是面包店。

张明：我要去面包店买面包和蛋糕。对了，我今天想理发。理发店在哪里？

陈丽丽：你很幸运！理发店就在面包店的左边。

张明：理发店的左边是什么店？

陈丽丽：那不是店，是银行。

张明：太好了！我们可以去银行取钱，然后我们可以去理发店理发，然后去面包店买面包和蛋糕，然后去小公园一边散步一边吃蛋糕，然后…

陈丽丽：然后去活动中心，是吗？

张明：为什么要去活动中心？

陈丽丽：因为活动中心有健身房，我们需要燃烧卡路里。

张明：我不喜欢去健身房。我们可以做其它运动，比如跑步和游泳。

陈丽丽：我没有去过我们的社区游泳池。我们今天下午去，好吗？

张明：我没有带泳裤。要不我们去活动中心打乒乓球，可以吗？

陈丽丽：也可以。打了乒乓球，我们可以去电影院看电影。

张明：好的！这个星期天太完美了！

Pinyin

Zhāng Míng: Wǒmen jīntiān qù nǎlǐ?

Chén Lìli: Nǐ kàn, zhè shì wǒmen shèqū de dìtú.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐmen shèqū hěn piàoliàng! Nǐmen shèqū yǒu hěn duō diànpù hé lóufáng ma?

Chén Lìli: Shìde, yǒu hěn duō. Nǐ kàn, zhèlǐ shì chāoshì hé diànyǐngyuàn. Diànyǐnyuàn zuǒbiān shì huódòng zhōngxīn hé shèqū yóuyǒngchí, yóuyǒngchí zuǒbiān shì xiǎo gōngyuán, gōngyuán zuǒbiān shì miànbāodiàn.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yào qù miànbāodiàn mǎi miànbāo hé dàngāo. Duìle, wǒ jīntiān xiǎng lǐfà. Lǐfàdiàn zàinǎlǐ?

Chén Lìli: Nǐ hěn xìngyùn! Lǐfàdiàn jiù zài miànbāodiàn de zuǒbiān.

Zhāng Míng: Lǐfàdiàn de zuǒbiān shì shénme diàn?

Chén Lìli: Nà bùshì diàn, shì yínháng.

Zhāng Míng: Tàihǎole! Wǒmen kěyǐ qù yínháng qǔqián, ránhòu wǒmen kěyǐ qù lǐfàdiàn lǐfà, ránhòu qù miànbāodiàn mǎi miànbāo hé dàngāo, ránhòu qù xiǎogōngyuán yībiān sànbù yībiān chī dàngāo, ránhòu…

Chén Lìli: Ránhòu qù huódòngzhōngxīn, shìma?

Zhāng Míng: Wèishénme yào qù huódòngzhōngxīn?

Chén Lìli: Yīnwèi huódòngzhōngxīn yǒu jiànshēnfáng, wǒmen xūyào ránshāo kǎlùlǐ.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ bù xǐhuān qù jiànshēnfáng. Wǒmen kěyǐ zuò qítā yùndòng, bǐrú pǎobù hé yóuyǒng.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ méiyǒu qùguò wǒmen de shèqū yóuyǒngchí, wǒmen jīntiān xiàwǔ qù, hǎo ma?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ měiyǒu dài yǒngkù. Yàobù wǒmen qù huódòngzhōngxīn dǎ pīngpāngqiú, kěyǐ ma?

Chén Lìli: Yě kěyǐ. Dǎle pīngpāngqiú, wǒmen kěyǐ qù diànyǐngyuàn kàn diànyǐng.

Zhāng Míng: Hǎode! Zhè gè xīngqītiān tài wánměi le!

English

Zhang Ming: Where are we going today?

Chen Lili: Look, this is the map of our community.

Zhang Ming: Your community is very pretty! Does your community have a lot of stores and buildings?

Chen Lili: Yes, it does. You see, here are the supermarket and theater. To the left of the theater are the club house and the community pool. To the left of the pool is a small park, and to the left of the park is the bakery.

Zhang Ming: I am going to buy bread and cake at the bakery. By the way, I want to have a haircut today. Where is the barbershop?

Chen Lili: You are very lucky! The barbershop is just to the left of the bakery.

Zhang Ming: What store is to the left of the barbershop?

Chen Lili: That is not a store; that’s a bank.

Zhang Ming: That’s great! We can go withdraw some money at the bank, then we can go have a haircut at the barbershop, then go buy bread and cake at the bakery, then go to the small park and have the cake while walking around, and then…

Chen Lili: Then we will go to the activity center, won’t we?

Zhang Ming: Why will we go to the activity center?

Chen Lili: Because there’s a gym at the activity center, and we need to burn those calories.

Zhang Ming: I don’t like to go to the gym. We can do other exercises, such as jogging and swimming.

Chen Lili: I’ve never been to our community pool. We can go this afternoon, okay?

Zhang Ming: I didn’t bring my swimming trunks. How about we go play ping-pong at the activity center?

Chen Lili: That’ll work. After ping-pong, we can then go watch a movie at the movie theater.

Zhang Ming: All right! This Sunday is too perfect!
