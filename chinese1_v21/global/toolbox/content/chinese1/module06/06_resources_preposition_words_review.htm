Preposition Words Review

Chinese Characters Chinese Pinyin English
左边 zuǒbiān left side
右边 yòubiān right side
上边 shàngbiān on top
下边 xiàbiān on bottom/under
旁边 pángbiān next to
后边 hòubiān behind
前边 qiánbiān in front of
角落 jiǎoluò corner

To describe the location of a place or object:

Subject + 在 zài (in/on/at) + preposition word

For example:

邮局在左边，药店在右边。 Yóujú zài zuǒbiān, yàodiàn zài yòubiān.

The post office is on the left side, and the pharmacy is on the right side.

To describe the location of a place or object, relative to the other:

Item #1 + 在 zài (in/on/at) + Item #2 + 的 de (of) + preposition word

For example:

邮局在药店的左边。 Yóujú zài yàodiàn de zuǒbiān.

The post office is on the left side of the pharmacy.
