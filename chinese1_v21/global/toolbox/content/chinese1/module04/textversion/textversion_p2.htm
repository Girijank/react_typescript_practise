Chinese Characters

陈丽丽：张明，你好！你什么时候开始上课？

张明：我下午一点上英文课，两点有音乐课，三点结束。

陈丽丽：你们英文课做什么？

张明：我们练习英文的听说读写。我现在会说英文，也会写英文。

陈丽丽：你真厉害！你们音乐课练习什么？

张明：我们练习唱歌。在你生日的时候，我唱生日歌给你听。

陈丽丽：我太高兴了！我要给你画一只小猫。

张明：好的。我很荣幸！

陈丽丽：今天上午有点凉。我穿了外套。

张明：我也穿了毛衣和夹克。

陈丽丽：下午天气会很暖和，你穿毛衣很热吧？

张明：不热。我不喜欢冷天。我从不穿短裤和凉鞋。

陈丽丽：我正好相反。我最喜欢穿裙子。

张明：你冷天在学校穿什么制服？

陈丽丽：在学校冷的时候，我头上戴帽子，上身穿毛衣或是外套，下身穿裙子。

张明：你们女孩真厉害！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Zhāng Míng, nǐhǎo! Nǐ shénme shíhòu kāishǐ shàngkè?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ xiàwǔ yīdiǎn shàng yīngwénkè, liǎngdiǎn yǒu yīnyuèkè, sāndiǎn jiéshù.

Chén Lìli: Nǐmen yīngwénkè zuò shénme?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒmen liànxí yīngwénde tīngshuōdúxiě. Wǒ xiànzài huì shuō yīngwén, yěhuì xiě yīngwén.

Chén Lìli: Nǐ zhēnlìhài! Nǐmen yīnyuèkè liànxí shénme?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒmen liànxí chànggē. Zài nǐ shēngrì de shíhòu, wǒ chàng shēngrìgē gěi nǐ tīng.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ tài gāoxìng le! Wǒ yào gěi nǐ huà yīzhī xiǎomāo.

Zhāng Míng: Hǎode. Wǒ hěn róngxìng!

Chén Lìli: Jīntiān shàngwǔ yǒudiǎn liáng. Wǒ chuān le wàitào.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yě chuānle máoyī hé jiākè.

Chén Lìli: Xiàwǔ tiānqì huì hěn nuǎnhuo, nǐ chuān máoyī hěnrè ba?

Zhāng Míng: Bù rè. Wǒ bùxǐhuān lěngtiān. Wǒ cóng bù chuān duǎnkù hé liángxié.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ zhènghǎoxiāngfǎn. Wǒ zuì xǐhuān chuān qúnzi.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ lěngtiān zài xuéxiào chuān shénme zhìfú?

Chén Lìli: Zài xuéxiào lěng de shíhòu, wǒ tóushàng dài màozi, shàngshēn chuān máoyī huòshì wàitào, xiàshēn chuān qúnzi.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐmen nǚhái zhēnlìhài!

English

Chen Lili: Hello, Zhang Ming! When do you start class?

Zhang Ming: I attend English class at 1 p.m., and music class at 2 p.m., ending at 3 p.m.

Chen Lili: What do you do in English class?

Zhang Ming: We practice English listening, speaking, reading, and writing. I now know how to speak

English, and I also know how to write English.

Chen Lili: You are really something! What do you practice in music class?

Zhang Ming: We practice singing. When it’s your birthday, I will sing the birthday song for you.

Chen Lili: I am so happy! I will draw a kitty cat for you.

Zhang Ming: Okay. I am very honored!

Chen Lili: It’s a little chilly this morning. I wore a coat.

Zhang Ming: I also wore a sweater and jacket.

Chen Lili: It will be very warm this afternoon. Won’t you feel very hot with a sweater?

Zhang Ming: No. I don’t like it cold. I never wear shorts or sandals.

Chen Lili: I am the exact opposite. I like to wear skirts the most.

Zhang Ming: What uniform do you wear at school on cold days?

Chen Lili: At school when it’s cold, I wear a hat on my head, a sweater or coat on the upper body, and a skirt on the lower body.

Zhang Ming: You girls are really something!
