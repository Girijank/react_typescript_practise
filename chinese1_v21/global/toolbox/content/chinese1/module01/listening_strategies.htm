Listening Strategies

  • Listen to the audio first without reading the text version. Then listen to it again while reading the text.
  • Listen for vocabulary you have learned or cognates you can recognize.
  • Don't worry if you don't understand everything at first. Use context clues to help with comprehension.
  • Read the questions ahead of time to help guide your listening.
  • Ask yourself comprehension questions as you listen. (Do I really understand that sentence?)
  • Pause the audio when necessary. You can also replay it as many times as you need to.
