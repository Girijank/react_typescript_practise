Chinese Character Writing Rules

Writing Rules Examples
Radicals in a Chinese character are written in order of “top to bottom” and “left to right.”

高gāo (tall)—top to bottom


你nǐ (you)—left to right
Write horizontal strokes, then vertical ones.

十 shí (ten)
Write left-falling strokes, then right-falling ones.

八 bā (eight)
For “inside and outside” characters, the outside frame is closed last.

国 guó (nation)
For a symmetrical character, draw the middle first, then left, then right.

水 shuǐ (water)
