Chinese Characters

陈丽丽: 你家有几个人？

张明: 我家有四个人，我爸爸，我妈妈，我姐姐和我。你家有谁

陈丽丽: 我家有我爸爸，我妈妈，我两个弟弟和我。

张明: 你没有姐姐吗？

陈丽丽: 我没有姐姐。

张明: 你有宠物吗？

陈丽丽: 我没有宠物。我妈妈不喜欢宠物。我很想有一只猫。你呢？

张明: 我有两只狗。一只大狗和一只小狗。我的小狗有点凶。

陈丽丽: 你看，这是我爷爷和奶奶。我爷爷秃顶。他很开朗。

张明: 你奶奶是她吗？她戴着眼镜，看起来很有智慧。

陈丽丽: 是的。他们有三个孩子，一个儿子和两个女儿。

张明: 他们的儿子是你爸爸，是吗？

陈丽丽: 是的。我外公和外婆有一个女儿，你说她是谁？

张明: 她一定是一个善良的女士。

陈丽丽: 你也很有智慧。

张明: 不敢当！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Nǐjiā yǒu jǐgè rén?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒjiā yǒu sìgè rén, wǒ bàba, wǒ māma, wǒ jiějie hé wǒ. Nǐjiā yǒu shéi?

Chén Lìli: Wǒjiā yǒu wǒ bàba, wǒ māma, wǒ liǎnggè dìdi hé wǒ

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ méiyǒu jiějie ma?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ méiyǒu jiějie.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ yǒu chǒngwù ma?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ méiyǒu chǒngwù. Wǒ māma bù xǐhuān chǒngwù. Wǒ hěn xiǎng yǒu yīzhī māo. Nǐne?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yǒu liǎng zhī gǒu. Yī zhī dàgǒu hé yī zhī xiǎogǒu. Wǒde xiǎogǒu yǒudiǎn xiōng.

Chén Lìli: Nǐ kàn, zhè shì wǒ yéye hé nǎinai. Wǒ yéye tūdǐng. Tā hěn kāilǎng.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ nǎinai shì tā ma? Tā dàizhe yǎnjìng, kànqǐlái hěn yǒu zhìhuì.

Chén Lìli: Shìde. Tāmen yǒu sāngè háizi, yīgè érzi hé liǎnggè nüér.

Zhāng Míng: Tāmen de érzi shì nǐ bàba, shìma?

Chén Lìli: Shìde. Wǒ wàigōng hé wàipó yǒu yīgè nüér, nǐ shuō tā shìshéi?

Zhāng Míng: Tā yīdìng shì yīgè shànliáng de nǚshì.

Chén Lìli: Nǐ yě hěn yǒu zhìhuì.

Zhāng Míng: Bùgǎndāng!

English

Chen Lili: How many people are in your family?

Zhang Ming: There are four people in my family: my dad, my mom, my older sister, and me. Who is in your family?

Chen Lili: My family has my dad, my mom, my two younger brothers, and me.

Zhang Ming: Do you have older sisters?

Chen Lili: I don’t have older sisters.

Zhang Ming: Do you have pets?

Chen Lili: I don’t have pets. My mom doesn’t like pets. I want to have a cat very much. What about you?

Zhang Ming: I have two dogs—one big dog and one small dog. My small dog is a little mean.

Chen Lili: You see, this is my paternal grandpa and grandma. My grandpa is bald. He is very outgoing.

Zhang Ming: Is she your paternal grandma? She is wearing glasses and looks very wise.

Chen Lili: Yes. They have three children: one son and two daughters.

Zhang Ming: Their son is your dad, isn’t he?

Chen Lili: Yes. My maternal grandpa and grandma have one daughter. Who do you say she is?

Zhang Ming: She must be a very kind lady.

Chen Lili: You are also very wise!

Zhang Ming: I’m sure don’t deserve that!
