Chinese Characters

陈丽丽：你今天上午要做什么？

张明：我要做家务。我要拖饭厅的地板，整理卧室，衣柜和壁橱，叠衣服，还要擦客厅的电扇，书架和茶几。

陈丽丽：好多家务！你下午可以休息了，坐在沙发上看电视。

张明：不可以。我下午要打扫厨房。我要整理冰箱，擦炉子和桌子。

陈丽丽：然后就可以休息了？

张明：还是不可以。我妈妈不在家，我要做饭。

陈丽丽：太累了！你晚餐要多吃米饭。

张明：我可能一边吃米饭一边就睡着了。你今天要做什么？

陈丽丽：我也差不多。我要打扫浴室。我要擦镜子，淋浴间，浴缸和马桶。

张明：也很累。下午呢？

陈丽丽：下午我要洗衣服。不过我没有洗衣液了，我需要去买。

张明：我和你一起去。我也需要买卫生纸和洗碗液。

陈丽丽：好啊。我正好也可以去倒垃圾。

张明：你是勤劳的小蜜蜂!

陈丽丽：我不是蜜蜂。我是漂亮的女孩。

张明：好吧。你是勤劳和漂亮的女孩！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Nǐ jīntiān shàngwǔ yào zuò shénme?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yào zuò jiāwù. Wǒ yào tuō fàntīng de dìbǎn, zhěnglǐ wòshì, yīguì hé bìchú, dié yīfu, háiyào cā kètīng de diànshàn, shūjià hé chájī.

Chén Lìli: Hǎoduō jiāwù! Nǐ xiàwǔ kěyǐ xiūxí le, zuò zài shāfā shàng kàn diànshì.

Zhāng Míng: Bù kěyǐ. Wǒ xiàwǔ yào dǎsǎo chúfáng. Wǒ yào zhěnglǐ bīngxiāng, cā lúzi hé zhuōzi.

Chén Lìli: Ránhòu jiù kěyǐ xiūxí le?

Zhāng Míng: Háishì bù kěyǐ. Wǒ māma bù zàijiā, wǒ yào zuòfàn.

Chén Lìli: Tài lèi le! Nǐ wǎncān yào duōchī mǐfàn.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ kěnéng yībiān chī mǐfàn yībiān jiù shuìzháole. Nǐ jīntiān yào zuò shénme?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ yě chàbùduō. Wǒ yào dǎsǎo yùshì. Wǒ yào cā jìngzi, línyùjiān, yùgāng hé mǎtǒng.

Zhāng Míng: Yě hěn lèi. Xiàwǔ ne?

Chén Lìli: Xiàwǔ wǒ yào xǐyīfú. Búguò wǒ méiyǒu xǐyīyè le, wǒ xūyào qù mǎi.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ hé nǐ yīqǐ qù. Wǒ yě xūyào mǎi wèishēngzhǐ hé xǐwǎnyè.

Chén Lìli: Hǎo a! Wǒ zhènghǎo yě kěyǐ qù dào lājī

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ shì qínláo de xiǎomìfēng.

Chén Lìli: Wǒ bùshì mìfēng. Wǒ shì piàoliàng de nǚhái.

Zhāng Míng: Hǎoba, nǐshì qínláo hé piàoliàng de nǚhái.

English

Chen Lili: What are you going to do this afternoon?

Zhang Ming: I need to do some chores. I need to mop the dining room floor, tidy up the bedroom, dresser and closet, fold clothes, and wipe the fan, bookshelf, and end table in the living room.

Chen Lili: So many chores! You can rest in the afternoon, watching TV while sitting on the sofa.

Zhang Ming: I can’t. In the afternoon, I need to clean the kitchen. I need to tidy up the fridge and wipe the stove and the table.

Chen Lili: Then you can rest?

Zhang Ming: Still can’t. My mom’s not home, and I need to cook.

Chen Lili: Too tiring! You need to eat a lot of rice for dinner.

Zhang Ming: I’ll probably fall asleep while eating rice. What are you going to do today?

Chen Lili: I’m doing about the same. I need to clean the bathroom. I need to wipe the mirror, shower area, bathtub, and toilet.

Zhang Ming: Also very tiring. What about in the afternoon?

Chen Lili: In the afternoon, I need to do laundry. But I am out of laundry detergent; I need to go buy some.

Zhang Ming: I’ll go with you. I also need to buy some toilet paper and dish soap.

Chen Lili: All right. I can also use the opportunity to take the trash out.

Zhang Ming: You are a busy little bee!

Chen Lili: I am not a bee. I’m a pretty girl.

Zhang Ming: All right. You are a diligent and pretty girl!
