总结 Zǒngjié Recap

Let’s quickly review what you’ve learned in this module so far. Here’s a checklist that you can go through very quickly for a little recap.

  • Say different parts of a house or apartment.
  • Say different furniture items in a bedroom and related chores.
  • Say different furniture items in a kitchen or a living room and related chores.
  • Ask and tell the composition of a house, using 多少 duōshǎo (how many) or 几 jǐ (how many (answered with small numbers)).
  • Use preposition words to describe the locations of houses or rooms.
  • Use 有 yǒu (to have) to describe the existence of furniture in a room.
  • Use 要 yào (to want, to be going to) to introduce chores to do.
  • Describe relative positions of furniture items, using the formula “item #1 + 在 zài (at) + item #2 + 的 de (of) + preposition word.”
  • Describe the existence of a furniture item with its position, using the formula “item #1 + 的 de (of) + preposition word + 有 yǒu (to have) + item #2.”
  • Understand different types of homes in China.
  • Understand the basic concepts of feng shui.
  • Know the feng shui rules for the inside and outside of a house and its location.
  • Know the feng shui rules for the bedroom.
  • Know the feng shui rules for the kitchen and the living room.
  • Know about the Chinese Farmers Almanac, including its origin, function in the past and in the present, and how to use it.
  • Understand cooking methods, utensils, and cooking routines in China.
  • Know pinyin components you’ve learned so far (also remember to practice the tones).
    • initials: f, y, d
    • finals: ian, ui, iang, ia

English

Minyi: Does your family live in a house or an apartment?

Tingting: My family lives in an apartment.

Minyi: Which floor do you live on? How many balconies does your home have?

Tingting: We live on the 6th floor. My home has two balconies.

Minyi: What does your bedroom have?

Tingting: My bedroom has a bed. To the left side of the bed are a nightstand and a lamp; to the right side is a dresser.

Minyi: What chores do you need to do in the bedroom?

Tingting: I need to tidy up the dresser and fold clothes in the bedroom.

Minyi: What does the kitchen in your home have?

Tingting: The kitchen in my home has a fridge, stove, and sink.

Minyi: What chores do you need to do in the kitchen?

Tingting: I need to cook and also wash dishes in the kitchen.
