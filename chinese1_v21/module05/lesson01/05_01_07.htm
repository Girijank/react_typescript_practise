Notes

  • In the words 左边 zuǒbiān (left side) and 右边 yòubiān (right side), the character 边 biān means “side.” Later in this module, you will learn more preposition words with this character in them.
  • The words 左边 zuǒbiān (left side) and 右边 yòubiān (right side) can be used after an item and a 的 de to describe a location. For example, you can use 门的右边 Mén de yòubiān (to the right of the door); 卧室的左边是浴室。Wòshì de zuǒbiān shì yùshì. (to the left of the bedroom is the bathroom).
  • The first character, 窗 chuāng, in 窗户 chuānghù (window) can also be used by itself with the same meaning— “window.”
