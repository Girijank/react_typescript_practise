Basic Concepts of Feng Shui

  • Feng shui is an ancient Taoism philosophy and its practice of planning and arranging a living space to reach a balance with the natural world.
  • Practicing feng shui involves using an octagon-shaped map called a 八卦 Bāguà (bagua). It indicates the distribution of different energies and how they flow.
  • The study of feng shui involves the five elements: earth, wood, metal, water, and fire and the aspects of human lives they represent.
