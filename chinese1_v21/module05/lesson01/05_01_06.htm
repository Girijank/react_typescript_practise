用起来 Yòng qǐlái Apply It!

Wonderful job learning about the feng shui rules! Now it is time to put them in practical use.

Examine the location, surrounding, and interior of your home. Does it follow any of the rules introduced? Identify at least one feng shui rule that it follows, and one that it doesn’t. For the one that it doesn’t follow, discuss with your parents how it can be improved. Make changes in or around the house with your parents if possible. If not, you can make a sketch of an ideal solution together. That can be used for your future house hunting!
