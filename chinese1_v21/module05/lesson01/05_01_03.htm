Notes

The word 楼 lóu (floor/story/building) can be used to describe floors/stories of a building by placing a number before it. You could say for example, 一楼 yīlóu (first floor) and 四楼 sìlóu (fourth floor). It also refers to a building. For example, you could use 大楼 dàlóu (big building) and 公寓楼 gōngyùlóu (apartment building).

It's Also Said

Another word for “floor/story” is 层 céng, whose original meaning is “layer.” For example, you would say 一层 yīcéng (first floor) and 四层 sìcéng (fourth floor).
