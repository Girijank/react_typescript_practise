准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Terrific job learning about describing your bedroom and some related chores. How wonderful! What about the other parts of your home? Imagine you have house guests from China, and you are giving them a tour around the living room and kitchen. How will you describe the furniture items your house guests see, and how will you describe some activities or chores you do in these areas? Let’s learn all about this and put your learning in action. Let’s have some fun in the house!

After this lesson, you will be able to give a nice house tour to your international guest. Totally awesome!
