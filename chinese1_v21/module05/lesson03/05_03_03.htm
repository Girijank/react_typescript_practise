Borrowed Words

沙发 shāfā (sofa) is a borrowed word from English, and its pronunciation in Chinese is similar to “sofa” in English.

Use the list of borrowed words to review words you’ve learned and others that you will learn later in this course.

Note

The verb 看 kàn (to watch/to look at) can be generally used for activities involving watching, seeing, or looking at something. For example: 看书 kànshū (read a book), 看电脑 kàn diànnǎo (look at the computer), and 看菜单 kàncàidān (look at the menu).
