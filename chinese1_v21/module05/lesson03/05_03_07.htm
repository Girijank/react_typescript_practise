Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio, and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need, to sound like a native speaker!




Notes

  • In the word 做饭 zuòfàn (to cook), the first character, 做 zuò, means “to do/to make.” You can use it for different nouns. For example: 做作业 zuò zuòyè (to do homework), 做蛋糕 zuò dàngāo (to make cake), and 做晚餐 zuòwǎncān (making dinner).
  • In both words 洗碗 xǐwǎn (to wash dishes) and 洗碗液 xǐwǎnyè (dish soap), the first character, 洗 xǐ, means “to wash.” For example: 洗袜子 xǐ wàzi (to wash socks), and 洗玻璃杯 xǐ bōlibēi (to wash a glass).
