My Mom’s Home Cooking Routine

  • Prep— Rinse the rice, clean and chop the vegetables, and clean, chop, and marinate the meat with seasoning.
  • Cook— Put the rice in the rice cooker, and put the chopped vegetables and meat in the wok/pot/pan.
  • Serve — Use plates or deep bowls for dishes, and use small bowls, chopsticks, and spoons for individuals.
