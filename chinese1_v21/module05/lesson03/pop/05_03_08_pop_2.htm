Common Cooking Methods

Below are some cooking methods my mom and aunts often use to cook.

  • Baking — Use dry heat in the oven to cook the ingredients. This is commonly used or bakery items, such as cakes, pies, and tarts.
  • Roasting — Use dry heat and open flame in the oven to cook the ingredients. This is commonly used for meats or certain vegetables.
  • Boiling — Submerge the ingredients in boiling water until they are ready. It is commonly used for meals such as pasta.
  • Pan frying — Fry the ingredients in a shallowly-oiled frying pan. Flip frequently to cook both sides.
