My Mom’s Cooking Routine

  • Prep — Clean and marinate the meat with seasoning, and then clean and chop the vegetables.
  • Cook — Cook the meat and bulb/root vegetable, such as potatoes, in a saucepan or frying pan; some vegetables can be served raw with salad sauce.
  • Serve — Use large plates or bowls for the main dish or salad, and use plates, knives, forks, and spoons for individuals.
