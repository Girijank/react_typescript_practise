Common Cooking Methods

There are many cooking methods that my mom and grandmother use to cook. Here are the ones they use the most:

  • Stir-frying — Stir the ingredients (usually chopped or minced) with hot oil quickly till they’re ready.
  • Steaming — Heat the ingredients in an enclosed steamer until they’re ready.
  • Boiling — Submerge the ingredient in boiling water until it’s ready. It is commonly used for meals such as dumplings or noodles.
  • Braising — Submerge the ingredient in broth or hot water; boil first, and then simmer it for hours until it’s ready.
