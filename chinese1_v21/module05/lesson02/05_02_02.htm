声母韵母 Shēngmǔ yùnmǔ Initials and Finals

In this lesson, we will learn the initial “y” and final “ui.”

Initial “y”

This initial sounds like “y” in English as in “you” and “year.” It’s used in words such as “院子 yuànzi (yard)” and “右边 yòu biān (right side).”

Final “ui”

This initial sounds like the combination of “oo” in English as in “wool” and “ay” as in “pay.” It’s used in words such as “会 huì (to know how to)” and “最 zuì (the most).”
