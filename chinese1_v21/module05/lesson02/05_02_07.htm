布置房间 Bùzhì fángjiān Setting Up My Room

Now with more vocabulary on hand, you can further your knowledge on how to describe positions of furniture items in your bedroom or how you want them set up.


Describing Relative Positions of Items

You can describe the relative positions of two furniture items by using the structure:

Item #1 + 在 zài (at) + Item #2 + 的 de (of) + preposition word

Note:

  • The word 的 de (of) can be omitted.
  • In English, the preposition word is placed before item #2. In Chinese, the preposition word goes after an item, and the order can’t be switched.

For example:

枕头在床的上边。 Zhěntóu zài chuáng de shàngbiān. The pillow is on top of the bed.

你的狗在书桌的下边。 Nǐde gǒu zài shūzhuō de xiàbiān. Your dog is under the desk.

You can also replace item #2 with a pronoun or the name of a person to describe the person’s relative position to an item.

For example:

书桌在我的右边。 Shūzhuō zài wǒde yòubiān. The desk is to my right.

衣柜在我姐姐的左边 Yīguì zài wǒ jiějie de zuǒbiān. The dresser is to the left of my older sister.

Describe Existence with Positions

With the preposition words you just learned, you can also describe what’s in your bedroom with their relative positions by using the structure:

Item #1 + 的 de (of) + preposition word + 有 yǒu (to have) + item #2

For example:

床的上边有枕头和床单。 Chuáng de shàngbiān yǒu zhěntóu hé chuángdān. There are pillows and bedsheets on top of the bed.

The word 的 de (of) and the character 边 biān in the preposition word can be omitted together. However, this doesn’t apply when the preposition word is 左边 zuǒbiān (left side) or 右边 yòubiān (right side).

For example:

床上有枕头和床单。 Chuáng shàng yǒu zhěntóu hé chuángdān. There are pillows and bedsheets on top of the bed.

床的左边有床头柜。 Chuángde zuǒbiān yǒu chuángtóuguì. There is a nightstand to the left of the bed.

(In this sentence, 床的左边 chuángde zuǒbiān cannot be omitted as 床左 chuáng zuǒ.)
