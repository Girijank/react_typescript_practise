More Feng Shui Rules

My cousins’ bedroom also complies with some feng shui rules. For example:

  • The headboard of their bunk beds is not next to a window but placed against a wall, so they are protected from negative energies that might enter through the window, and the wall also brings security.
  • There’s a beam on the ceiling in their bedroom which they avoid placing their beds under, since beams symbolize evil oppression or disaster from overhead.
  • The bottom of their beds is lifted up from the floor, which protects them from the unpleasant spirits from the ground.
  • They have just one or two plants in their bedroom, since too many plants can bring too much yin energy, which can affect their health.
