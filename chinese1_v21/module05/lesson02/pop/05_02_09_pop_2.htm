Feng Shui Rules

Many bedroom feng shui rules are applied to my bedroom. For example:

  • My room is rectangular since irregular shapes may cause me to have tension.
  • My bedroom door doesn’t face the house entrance, so good luck, health, and fortune won’t escape.
  • My bedroom door doesn’t face the bathroom door, so any unwanted odors won’t enter.
  • My bedroom is not next to the kitchen, so the heat won’t make the room uncomfortable or too hot.
  • I hung a mirror at my bedroom door facing outwards, so it repels evil spirits.
