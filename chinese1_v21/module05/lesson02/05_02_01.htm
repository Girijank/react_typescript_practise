准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

You’ve just learned to describe houses and different sections in a house in Chinese. How awesome! You’ve also learned some basic feng shui rules applied to locations of a house or rooms. Now it’s time to get into more details. Imagine you are talking with your friend about your bedroom. How do you describe different furniture items in your room and how they are set up in Chinese? How do you say some of the chores you need to get done in the room? We’ll learn all of this. Now your conversation with your friend will become more and more interesting!

After this lesson, you can brag about your room in Chinese to friends in China. How cool!
