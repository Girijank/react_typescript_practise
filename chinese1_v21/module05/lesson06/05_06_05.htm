What are some common appliances seen in cities in China?

Fridge, stove, microwave, shower, toilet, and washing machine

Are takeout services popular in China, and why?

Takeout services are popular in China, especially among young people who have busy lives and need the convenience.

Is it common in rural China that parents are away in big cities? In that case, who does most of the household chores?

It is common in rural China that adults go to big cities to make more money. In that case, usually the oldest child in the family does most of the household chores.

What types of appliances are commonly seen in rural China and the rural U.S. respectively?

In rural China, appliances, such as televisions, fridges, and washing machines can be seen, but appliances, such as sit-on toilets and showers are not yet very common. People in rural areas in the U.S. have similar appliances as people living in cities, such as fridges, stoves, showers, toilets, washing machines and dryers, dishwashers, bathtubs, and garbage disposals.
