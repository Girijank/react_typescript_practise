Notes

In the word 淋浴间 línyùjiān (shower area), the third character, 间 jiān, means “room/space” in general.

It’s Also Said

There are different ways to say “bathroom” besides 浴室 yùshì, depending on the types or locations of bathrooms. For example: 卫生间 wèishēngjiān (commonly used for bathrooms in households), 洗手间 xǐshǒujiān (commonly used for bathrooms in restaurants), 厕所 cèsuǒ (commonly refers to public bathrooms). These words are often used interchangeably by people's preferences and there are no set rules on when to use which.
