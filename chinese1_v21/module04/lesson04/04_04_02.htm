Writing Rules

Review the following writing rules, with demonstrations of these rules, using characters you’ve learned in or before this module.

Radicals in a Chinese character are written in order of “top to bottom” and “left to right.”

要 yào —top to bottom


课 kè —left to right

Write horizontal strokes, then vertical ones.

书 shū

Write left-falling strokes, then right-falling ones.

会 huì

For “inside and outside” characters, the outside frame (completely or half-closed) is completed last.

画 huà

For a symmetrical character, draw the middle first, then left, then right.
