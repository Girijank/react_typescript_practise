声母韵母 Shēngmǔ yùnmǔ Initials and Finals

In this lesson we will learn initial “t” and final “u.”

Initial “t”

This initial sounds like “t” in English as in “to” or “take.” It’s used in words such as “汤 tāng (soup)” and “台湾 táiwān (Taiwan).”

Final “u”

This final sounds like “oo” in English as in “loose” or “tool.” It’s used in words such as “书 shū (book)” and “下午 xiàwǔ (p.m./afternoon).”
