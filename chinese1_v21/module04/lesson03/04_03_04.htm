数日子 Shǔ rìzi Counting Days

By now you are able to say dates in Chinese, including the year, month, and day. What about days of the week? Well, today’s the day to learn just that.

To say a day of the week, simply follow the structure: 星期 xīngqī (week) + a number (starting with number one for Monday).

Example:

  • 星期一 xīngqīyī (Monday)
  • 星期六 xīngqīliù (Saturday)

Notes

For Sunday, instead of adding the number seven, you need to add either 日 rì (day) or 天 tiān (day(casual)). So, Sunday is 星期日 xīngqīrì or 星期天 xīngqītiān.
