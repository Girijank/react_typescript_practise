演讲时间 Yǎnjiǎng shíjiān Presentational Speaking

It’s time to showcase your verbal skills on what you’ve learned. For this presentation, you will record an audio or a video, speaking in complete sentences in Chinese. Your audio or video should include the grammar and vocabulary from the lessons.

Using complete sentences in Chinese, talk about your favorite class at school. For the class, you need to describe who the teacher is, when the class starts and ends, at least two school supplies you need for this class, at least two activities you do in this class, and at least two clothes items you can wear to this class. Take the following steps to complete your description.

Note

  • You can describe a fictitious class. The teacher or other elements for this class don’t have to be from real life.
  • Use 最喜欢 zuì xǐhuān (to like … the most) to introduce your favorite class.
  • Use 可以 kěyǐ (may/can) to introduce the clothes you can wear to this class.
  • You can say your teacher’s name in English if he or she doesn’t have a Chinese name.
  • Review the classes and activities you’ve learned so far.
  • Review the school supplies you’ve learned so far.
  • Review the clothes items you’ve learned so far.

    2. Student Sample

    我最喜欢生物课。我的生物老师是李老师。我的生物课上午十点三刻开始，下午十二点一刻结束。上生物课我们需要彩笔和显微镜。我们观察洋葱表皮和画植物细胞。上生物课我可以穿实验服和戴手套。

    Wǒ zuì xǐhuān shēngwùkè. Wǒde shēngwù lǎoshī shì Lǐ Lǎoshī. Wǒde shēngwùkè shàngwǔ shídiǎn sānkè kāishǐ, xiàwǔ shièrdiǎn yīkè jiéshù. Shàng shēngwùkè wǒmen xūyào cǎibǐ hé xiǎnwēijìng. Wǒmen guānchá yángcōng biǎopí hé huà zhíwù xìbāo. Shàng shēngwù kè wǒ kěyǐ chuān shíyànfú hé dài shǒutào.

    I like biology class the most. My biology teacher is Teacher Li. My biology class starts at ten o’clock and three quarters a.m. and ends at twelve o’clock and one quarter p.m. For the biology class, we need colored pencils and a microscope. We observe onion peels and draw plant cells. For biology class, I can wear a lab coat and gloves.

  2. Know the requirements of your presentation.

    Note: The sample English sentences in parentheses are just a guide. You need to come up with your own original sentences in Chinese.

    • Say what your favorite class is. (e.g., My favorite class is biology class.)
    • Say who the teacher is. (e.g., My biology teacher is Teacher Li.)
    • Say when the class starts and ends. (e.g., My biology class starts at 10:45 a.m. and ends at 12:15 p.m.)
    • Say at least two school supplies you need for this class. (e.g., For the biology class, we need colored pencils and a microscope.)
    • Say at least two activities you do in this class. (e.g., We observe onion peels and draw plant cells.)
    • Say at least two clothes items you can wear to this class. (e.g., For biology class, I can wear a lab coat and gloves.)
  3. Finalize your oral presentation
    • Your recording should be about 15-30 seconds long.
    • Give your presentation in Chinese, using appropriate vocabulary and grammar.
    • Start your presentation by stating your first and last name in English. (e.g., My name is Angie Liu.)
    • Speak clearly and loudly enough for the microphone to record all your words.
    • Speak at an appropriate rate. (Don’t speak too quickly or too slowly, and do not take extended pauses.)
    • Use correct tones and pronunciations throughout the speech.
    • Practice a couple of times, first in a mirror, or in front of a family member or friend.

Congratulations! You are on your way to becoming a fluent Chinese speaker!
