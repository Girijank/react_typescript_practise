总结 Zǒngjié Recap

Let’s quickly review what you’ve learned in this module so far. Here’s a checklist that you can go through very quickly for a little recap.

  • Saying “a.m.” and “p.m.”
  • Saying different subjects and classes
  • Saying different school supplies
  • Planning on doing something with "要 yào (to want)"
  • Describing going somewhere with “去 qù (to go to)”
  • Telling time, including the hour and the minute
  • Using 需要 xūyào (to need) to describe needs of school supplies for classes
  • Using 或是 huòshì (or) in a statement and understanding its difference with 还是 háishì (or)
  • Refreshing knowledge on borrowed words in Chinese
  • Saying days of the week
  • Describing school schedule including times, classes and activities
  • Describing learned skills, using 会 huì (know how to)
  • Describing capabilities, using 能 néng (can/be able to)
  • Describing class activities, using “verb + object” structure
  • Understanding school dos and don’ts, using 不要 bùyào (do not)
  • Describing attending class, using 上 shàng (to attend (class))
  • Understanding class setup and learning focuses in Chinese schools
  • Describing campus life and classroom routines and setups
  • Knowing bell schedules and student lives in Chinese schools
  • Knowing pinyin components you’ve learned so far (also remember to practice the tones.):
    • initials: k, w, t
    • finals: a, üe, ang, u

小练习 Xiǎo Liànxí A Little Practice

Let’s practice before speaking with your instructor. Remember the Chinese saying 千里之行, 始于足下 Qiānlǐzhīxíng, shǐyúzúxià. (The journey of a thousand miles begins with one step.) The accumulation of your consistent hard work will take you far on your journey of language learning.

Read the scenario below; then listen to the questions. Answer the questions, using complete sentences in Chinese, based on the scenario. There are two options: Need help? and Need more help? This weekend, the two get together for a playdate, and 敏仪 Mǐnyí’s curious about 婷婷 Tíngting’s new school and asks her questions about it. 敏仪 Mǐnyí starts the conversation, and you will take the role of 婷婷 Tíngting to answer 敏仪 Mǐnyí’s questions, based on the information given below.

婷婷 Tíngting’s school starts at 8 a.m. every day, and school’s over at 3 p.m. She has math, Chinese, art, and music classes on Fridays. Chinese Characters

敏仪: 你们每天什么时候开始上课?什么时候结束?

婷婷: 我们每天上午八点开始上课，下午三点结束

敏仪: 你们上音乐课练习什么?

婷婷: 我们练习唱歌。

Pinyin

Mǐnyí: Nǐmen měitiān shénme shíhòu kāishǐ shàngkè? Shénme shíhòu jiéshù?

Tíngting: Wǒmen měitiān shàngwǔ bādiǎn kāishǐ shàngkè, xiàwǔ sāndiǎn jiéshù.

Mǐnyí: Nǐ xīngqīwǔ yǒu shénme kè?

Tíngting: Wǒ xīngqīwǔ yǒu shùxuékè, zhōngwénkè, yìshùkè, hé yīnyuèkè.

Mǐnyí: Nǐ shàng shùxuékè xūyào shénme?

Tíngting: Wǒ xūyào bǐjìběn, chǐzi, qiānbǐ huòshì bǐ.

Mǐnyí: Shàng zhōngwénkè nǐ yǒu zìdiǎn ma?

Tíngting: Wǒ méiyǒu zìdiǎn. Wǒ yǒu diànnǎo.

Mǐnyí: Shàng yìshùkè nǐ huì shénme?

Tíngting: Wǒ huì huà māo hé gǒu.

Mǐnyí: Nǐmen shàng yīnyuèkè liànxí shénme?

Tíngting: Wǒmen liànxí chànggē.

English

Minyi: When do you start school every day? When does it end?

Tingting: Every day our school starts at 8 a.m. and ends at 3 p.m.

Minyi: What classes do you have on Fridays?

Tingting: On Fridays I have math, Chinese, art, and music classes.

Minyi: What do you need for math class?

Tingting: I need a notebook, a ruler, pencils, or pens.

Minyi: Do you have a dictionary for Chinese class?

Tingting: I don’t have a dictionary. I have a computer.

Minyi: What do you learn to do in art class?

Tingting: I know how to draw cats and dogs.

Minyi: What do you practice in music class?

Tingting: We practice singing.
