Chinese Characters

陈丽丽： 你今天什么时候到的学校？

张明： 我上午八点到的。

陈丽丽： 你很准时！你吃午餐了吗？

张明： 我上午十一点四十五分吃了。你呢？

陈丽丽： 我也吃了。你今天下午有什么课？

张明： 我下午一点有英文课，两点是数学课，三点上体育课。你呢？

陈丽丽： 我下午一点是社会研究课，两点上中文课，三点也是体育课。

张明： 你上中文课需要字典或是电脑。你有字典吗？

陈丽丽： 哎呀！我没有带字典，也没有带电脑。

张明：你可以用我的字典。

陈丽丽： 谢谢你！你上英文课也需要字典吧？

张明： 没关系。我有电脑，电脑上有在线字典。

陈丽丽： 太好了！那我就放心了。你上数学课需要什么？

张明： 我需要笔记本，铅笔和尺子。你看我的背包，我都有。

陈丽丽： 你很有先见之明。

张明： 我也觉得我很有智慧。你是在体育馆上体育课吗？

陈丽丽： 是的。你呢？

张明： 我也是。我们下午三点见？

陈丽丽：好的，下午三点见！

Pinyin

Chén Lìli: Nǐ jīntiān shénme shíhòu dàode xuéxiào?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ shàngwǔ bādiǎn dàode.

Chén Lìli: Nǐ hěn zhǔnshí! Nǐ chī wǔcān le ma?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ shàngwǔ shíyīdiǎn sìshíwǔ fēn chīle. Nǐne?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ yě chīle. Nǐ jīntiān xiàwǔ yǒu shénme kè?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ xiàwǔ yīdiǎn yǒu yīngwénkè, liǎngdiǎn shì shùxuékè, sāndiǎn shàng tǐyùkè. Nǐne?

Chén Lìli: Wǒ xiàwǔ yīdiǎn shì shèhuìyánjiūkè, liǎngdiǎn shàng zhōngwénkè, sāndiǎn yěshì tǐyùkè.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ shàng zhōngwénkè xūyào zìdiǎn huòshì diànnǎo. Nǐ yǒu zìdiǎn ma?

Chén Lìli: Āiyā! Wǒ méiyǒu dài zìdiǎn, yě méiyǒu dài diànnǎo.

Zhāng Míng: Nǐ kěyǐ yòng wǒde zìdiǎn.

Chén Lìli: Xièxiènǐ! Nǐ shàng yīngwénkè yě xūyào zìdiǎn ba?

Zhāng Míng: Méiguānxi. Wǒ yǒu diànnǎo, diànnǎo shàng yǒu zàixiàn zìdiǎn.

Chén Lìli: Tàihǎole! Nà wǒ jiù fàngxīnle. Nǐ shàng shùxuékè xūyào shénme?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ xūyào bǐjìběn, qiānbǐ hé chǐzi. Nǐ kàn wǒde bèibāo, wǒ dōu yǒu.

Chén Lìli: Nǐ hěn yǒu xiānjiànzhīmíng.

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yě juéde wǒ hěn yǒuzhìhuì. Nǐ shì zài tǐyùguǎn shàng tǐyùkè ma?

Chén Lìli: Shìde. Nǐne?

Zhāng Míng: Wǒ yěshì. Wǒmen xiàwǔ sāndiǎn jiàn?

Chén Lìli: Hǎode. Xiàwǔ sāndiǎn jiàn!

English

Chen Lili: What time did you arrive at school today?

Zhang Ming: I arrived at 8 a.m.

Chen Lili: You are right on time! Did you eat lunch?

Zhang Ming: I had lunch at 11:45 a.m. What about you?

Chen Lili: I’ve also eaten. What classes do you have this afternoon?

Zhang Ming: I have English class at 1 p.m., math at 2, and PE at 3. What about you?

Chen Lili: I have social studies class at 1 p.m., Chinese class at 2, and I have PE class at 3 also.

Zhang Ming: For Chinese class, you need a dictionary or a computer. Do you have dictionary?

Chen Lili: Oh no! I didn’t bring a dictionary, nor did I bring a computer.

Zhang Ming: You may use my dictionary.

Chen Lili: Thank you! For English class you also need a dictionary, right?

Zhang Ming: That’s all right. I have a computer, and there’s an online dictionary on the computer.

Chen Lili: That’s great! Then I feel better. What do you need for math class?

Zhang Ming: I need a notebook, a pencil, and a ruler. Look at my backpack; I have them all.

Chen Lili: You are very good at thinking ahead.

Zhang Ming: I also feel I’m very wise. Do you have your PE class in the gym?

Chen Lili: Yes. What about you?

Zhang Ming: Mine, too. See you at 3 p.m.?

Chen Lili: Okay. See you at 3 p.m.
