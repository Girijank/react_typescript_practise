录影中! Lùyǐng zhōng! In Action!

It’s now time to see what you’ve learned in action. Watch the dialogue video to see how the vocabulary and structures you’ve learned so far can be used in real-life settings; then you can apply them in your own daily life.

Helpful Hints

When viewing the dialogue videos in this course, remember:

  • Watch each video twice. First, listen through it to see how much you can initially understand. Pay attention to the context and non-verbal cues, such as facial expressions, hand gestures, and body movements.
  • Don’t worry if you don’t understand everything during the first viewing. Make use of the closed captioning to help you understand. Pause the video when necessary so you can have a full view of the sentences in characters or pinyin.
  • Feel free to speak along with the dialogue in the video. This will help you build a strong sense of intonations, pronunciations, and cadence in Chinese speech.
  • Read the comprehension questions beforehand. Keep the questions in mind while watching the video.
  • Answer the questions in your note guide, and then check your answers. Note: You will have similar questions on the assessments, so be sure to take the time to practice now.

Why does Chen Lili want to draw Zhang Ming a kitty cat?

When they are discussing clothing for the afternoon, Chen Lili says “我正好相反。Wǒ zhènghǎoxiāngfǎn.” What do you think the phrase “正好相反 zhènghǎoxiāngfǎn“ means, according to the context?

Zhang Ming said he never wears shorts or sandals, while Chen Lili responds by saying she likes to wear skirts the most, which is the exact opposite, so the phrase should mean “the exact opposite.”
