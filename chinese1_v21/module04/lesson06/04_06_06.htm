Notes

By now you’ve learned three words with similar meanings: 会 huì (to know how to), 能 néng (can (to be able to)), and 可以 kěyǐ (may/can). Later in this lesson, you will learn when to use which one.

Borrowed Words

夹克 jiākè (jacket) is a borrowed word from English. Its pronunciation in Chinese is similar to “jacket” in English.
