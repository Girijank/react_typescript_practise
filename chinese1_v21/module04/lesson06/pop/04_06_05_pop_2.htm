Karen My School's Dress requirements

Left, a group of students standing together; middle, a student holding books smiling; right, cartoon image of Karen

  • Public schools here generally don’t require uniforms. We can wear comfortable and appropriate clothes to school. Private schools here usually require uniforms, which usually align with the school’s theme colors.
  • During summer, we can wear short-sleeved skirts and shorts/skirts to school. During winter, we wear overcoats.
  • We can wear accessories such as earrings and bracelets to school. We can dye and/or decorate our hair.
  • We mostly wear sneakers to school. Sandals are allowed in some schools. Slippers are generally not allowed on campus no matter how tired we are!
