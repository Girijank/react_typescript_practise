On-Campus Behavior

Left, students raising hands in class; middle, a teacher with headset smiling and waving; right, cartoon image of Karen

  • We have locker rooms where we change for PE classes. There are usually showers in the locker room where we can take showers after PE classes.
  • We have rules for on-campus behavior too. Some of them are very similar to yours! For example:
    • We should raise hands if we wish to speak during class, and we can talk during discussion time, too.
    • We should sit attentively during classes.
    • We greet our teacher when we walk into his or her classroom.
    • We greet a teacher when we run into him or her on campus.
