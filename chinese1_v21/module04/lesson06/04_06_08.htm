Notes

In the word 凉鞋 liángxié (sandals), the first character 凉 liáng means “cool.” It can also be used to describe the weather, as opposed to 暖和 nuǎnhuo (warm).

It’s Also Said

Remember the adjectives 热 rè (hot) and 冷 lěng (cold)? They can also be used to describe the weather. With these two, you can say 热天 rètiān (hot days) and 冷天 lěngtiān (cold days).
