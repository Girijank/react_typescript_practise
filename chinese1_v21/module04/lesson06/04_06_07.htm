获得允许 Huòdé yǔnxǔ Getting Permissions

By now you’ve learned three verbs that have similar meanings: 会 huì (to know how to), 能 néng (can/to be able to), and 可以 kěyǐ (may/can). These words sometimes have overlapping meanings.

  • 会 huì (to know how to): ability (mostly through learning);
  • 能 néng (can/to be able to): ability (mostly born with); permission; request; possibility
  • 可以 kěyǐ (may/can): permission; request; possibility

Often there are no clear-cut lines between the use of these three verbs. They can be used interchangeably—but not always. You will accumulate case-by-case situations as you go to help you decide which verb to use. For now, you can use these tips to help:

  • 会 huì (to know how to)—an action you need to learn or be trained to do, such as speaking a language, skating, and knitting

    Example:

    我哥哥会说中文。 Wǒ gēge huì shuō zhōngwén. (My older brother knows how to speak Chinese.)

  • 能 néng (can/to be able to)—a born physical ability or natural talent, such as eating, drinking, and lifting a certain weight

    Example:

    他午餐能吃三个汉堡。 Tā zǎocān néng chī sāngè hànbǎo. (He can eat three hamburgers for lunch.)

  • 可以 kěyǐ (may/can)—an action for which you need permission of a person or a situation, such as being allowed to speak in class or wearing a skirt when it’s hot

    Example:

    你在春天可以穿毛衣。 Nǐ zài chūntiān kěyǐ chuān máoyī. (In the spring, you can wear a sweater.)


Notes

You can add 不 bù (not) before 可以 kěyǐ (may/can). For example: 你不可以穿凉鞋去学校。 Nǐ bùkěyǐ chuān liángxié qù xuéxiào. (You cannot wear sandals to school.)

Let’s practice the use of these three words.

Self-Check

Select the word that best completes the sentence. Select each arrow to review the answer to each question.

你上课不_____说话。

A) 会
B) 能
C) 可以

C) 可以

你上课不可以说话。 Nǐ shàngkè bùkěyǐ shuōhuà. You can’t talk during class.

她姐姐_____写中文。

A) 会
B) 能
C) 可以

A) 会

她姐姐会写中文。 Tā jiějie huì xiě zhōngwén. Her older sister knows how to write in Chinese.

你在热天_____穿裙子。

A) 会
B) 能
C) 可以

C) 可以

你在热天可以穿裙子。 Nǐ zài rètiān kěyǐ chuān qúnzi. You can wear a skirt on hot days.

我每天_____喝五杯水。

A) 会
B) 能
C) 可以

B) 能

我每天能喝五杯水。 Wǒ měitiān néng hē wǔ bēi shuǐ. I can drink five glasses of water every day.
