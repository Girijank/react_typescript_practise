Notes

或 huò, the first character of 或是 huòshì (or), can be used by itself, also meaning “or.” For example, both 苹果或橙子 píngguǒ huò chéngzi and 苹果或是橙子 píngguǒ huòshì chéngzi mean “apple or orange.”
