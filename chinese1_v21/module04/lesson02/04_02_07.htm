班会话事 Bānhuì huàshì Student Committee Calls It

或是 huòshì (or) or 还是 háishì (or)

So far, you’ve learned two words that means “or”: 或是 huòshì and 还是 háishì. Do you know when to use which one?

  • 或是 huòshì is used when using “or” in a statement. For example:

我用筷子或是勺子吃米饭。 Wǒ yòng kuàizi huòshì sháozi chī mǐfàn. I use chopsticks or a spoon to eat rice.

我上英文课需要铅笔或马克笔。Wǒ shàng yīng wénkè xūyào qiānbǐ huò mǎkèbǐ. To attend English class, I need a pencil or a marker.

  • 还是 háishì is used when providing choices in a question. For example:

你喝咖啡还是喝热茶？Nǐ hē kāfēi háishì hē rèchá? Do you drink coffee or drink hot tea?
