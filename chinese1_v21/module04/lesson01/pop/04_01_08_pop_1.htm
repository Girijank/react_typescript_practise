Time schedule

Kailan

  • We normally start school around 7:30 a.m.
  • We have four 45-minute classes in the morning, and then we have a long lunch break from 12 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. Many students use this time to take a nap. Then we have classes again from 2:30 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.
  • Some schools also offer evening study time in the classrooms during exam seasons.

Karen

  • We also start school as early as 7:30 a.m.
  • We have five to six 45-minute classes (or as we call them, periods) till 2:10 p.m. Around 12 p.m., we have around 30 minutes for a lunch break but no nap time.
  • Some students use the last period at school as study hall. We mostly have evening study time at home.
