Classroom set up

Kailan

Image of Chinese students in red, standing straight by their desks.

  • At my school, students stay in the same classroom all day. Teachers prepare lessons in their offices, and then go to different classrooms to teach.
  • Students are responsible for the cleaning and decoration of the classrooms. Every Friday afternoon, all the students tidy up their classrooms and have a competition on which classroom is the cleanest.
  • Because classmates spend time together in the same classroom, we have very close relationships that last a lifetime.

  • The teachers usually decorate their classrooms with posters related to the subject. We have school janitors who clean up the classrooms at the end of each school day.
