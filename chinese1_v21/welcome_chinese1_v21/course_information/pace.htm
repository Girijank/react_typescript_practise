Pace

In certain online courses, you are able to determine your own schedule, forward momentum, or pace. As an online student, you are expected to follow your course's pace and submit activities for grading in a timely manner.

Think about which pace would be right for you. Talk to your parent(s) or guardian(s), guidance counselor, and instructor to help you make the best decision.

FLVS Full-Time Students

The pace options below do not apply to students enrolled in the FLVS Full-Time Program. To see your assignments and due dates for the semester, select the Calendar link in your course.

Pace Charts

Choose a pace for completing this course from the options below.

Print and fill in the appropriate pace guide and place it in your work area for future reference. It is recommended that you give a copy to your parent(s), guardian(s), or your learning facilitator.

Standard Pace Options

  • Traditional Pace: Complete the course in 32–36 weeks. This pace is recommended.
  • Accelerated Pace: Complete the course in 16–18 weeks.
  • Second Segment Only Pace: Complete the course in one segment (16–18 weeks).
    Note: This option is for students who are only seeking credit for the second segment and did not take the first segment online with this school.

Individual Pace Planner

The Individual Pace Planner is a template that allows the teacher or student to input information to formulate a completion plan for the course. This can be based on planned hours in the course per week or start and end dates. This planner will generate a schedule that outlines what assessments should be completed each week to accomplish the goals set.
