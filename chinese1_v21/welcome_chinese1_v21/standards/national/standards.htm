National Standards

National Standards
Module National Standards
Module One
  • ACTFL 1.1: Students engage in conversations, provide and obtain information, express feelings and emotions, and exchange opinions.
  • ACTFL 1.2: Students understand and interpret spoken and written information in the target language on a variety of topics.
  • ACTFL 1.3: Students present information, concepts, and ideas in the target language to an audience of listeners or readers on a variety of topics.
  • ACTFL 2.1: Students demonstrate an understanding of the relationship between the practices and perspectives of the target culture.
  • ACTFL 2.2: Students demonstrate an understanding of the relationship between the products and perspectives of the target culture.
  • ACTFL 3.1: Students reinforce and further their knowledge of other disciplines through the target language.
  • ACTFL 3.2: Students acquire information and recognize the distinctive viewpoints that are only available through the target language and its cultures.
  • ACTFL 4.1: Students demonstrate understanding of the nature of language through comparisons between target language and own.
  • ACTFL 4.2: Students demonstrate the understanding of the concept of culture through comparisons of the cultures studied and their own.
  • ACTFL 5.1: Students use the target language both within and beyond the school setting.
  • ACTFL 5.2: Students show evidence of becoming life-long learners by using the target language for personal enjoyment and enrichment.
