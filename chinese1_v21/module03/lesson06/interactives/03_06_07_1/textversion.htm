How long is the Spring Festival celebration?

Its celebration lasts for 15 days, from the 1st day of the lunar new year to the 15th day. The 15th day is also known as the Lantern Festival.

Who does the Dragon Boat Festival commemorate and why?

The Dragon Boat Festival commemorates the famous scholar in Chinese history, Qu Yuan, for his patriotism. He drowned himself in the Miluo River in honor of his nation.

Name two activities for celebration of Moon Festival.

Any two of the following:

  • Have family reunion dinner.
  • Eat mooncakes known as “yuebing.”
  • Appreciate the full moon.
  • Make colorful lanterns.
  • Play riddles or recite poems.

What do the foods eaten during the Winter Solstice function as or symbolize?

Various types of foods are eaten during the Winter Solstice to symbolize togetherness of family and bring warmth to the body.
