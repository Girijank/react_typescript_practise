特殊的日子 Tèshū de rìzi Special Day

What’s the most special day just for you of the whole year? That’s right. Your birthday! You might be wondering how to tell people when your birthday is and how to ask a friend when his or her birthday is. You are about to equip your Chinese vocabulary with the words related to this topic.

Note: You may practice writing the characters of the words or phrases in your note guide, but you will not be assessed on writing them.

Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need to sound like a native speaker!


Notes

When introducing a birthday, you can either say year, month, and day or just month and day. On the next page, you will learn how to say dates in Chinese.

打个卡 Dǎgèkǎ Checkpoint

There is a Chinese saying 日积月累 rì jī yuè lěi. (Accumulation takes days and months.) Continue accumulating your vocabulary, and day by day, month by month, you will see the difference.
