准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Let’s say your next step in your Chinese adventure is to start a blog, which may be viewed by Chinese-speaking people all over the world. What do you post about? Childhood is always a favorite topic, and what better way to start than with your favorite memories from childhood birthday parties? How do you ask your followers when their birthdays are? By the end of this lesson, you will be able to do just that!

In this lesson, you will also meet Wanting Zhong, a middle school student from Harbin, China, and learn about her life and cultural surroundings.

Are you ready? Let’s go!
