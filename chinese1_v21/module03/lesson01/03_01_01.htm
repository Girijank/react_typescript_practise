准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready To Go?

By now, you’ve tackled two modules in this course. You’ve accumulated quite an amount of vocabulary, and you are now able to carry on some conversations in Chinese. You are on a roll! Now, let’s further your learning even more. Have you ever wondered if introducing your family is different in Chinese? Do you have all the vocabulary you need to address different family members? Or maybe you have a friend who speaks Chinese. How would you ask about his or her family? In this module, you will be equipped with the necessary vocabulary words and structures so you can start and carry on a conversation on such topics. You are getting there!
