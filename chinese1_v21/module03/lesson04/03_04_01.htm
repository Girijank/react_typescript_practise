Have you seen a family tree before? Every family has its lineage trace, which is like the tree trunk, while members of the family that connect to each other by blood and by love are like leaves on the tree to make it prosperous and evergreen. Have you drawn a family tree of your own with at least three generations included—your grandparents, your parents, and your own generation? In this lesson, you will learn to create a family tree in Chinese characters. With this knowledge, you will be able to create a family tree of your own, which can be a beautiful gift to your family, made with love. It can even be hung up in your family living room!
