How many senior citizens is China projected to have by 2050?

China will have 400 million citizens who are 60 or older by 2050.

How is the traditional family-based senior care changing with time?

Because of the fast-paced modern lifestyle, more seniors are living separately from their children, and children are sometimes too busy to take care of their elderly.

What are some solutions for the increasing need of housing for China’s senior citizens?

China is encouraging the participation of private and foreign investments into China’s senior housing market. The government is also making its policies clearer and more specific.

How do the societies and communities in China help their senior citizens?

They combine the efforts of families, communities, and society to provide a network of physical and mental services for senior citizens. Some cities also build more senior centers to provide medical care and entertainment for their seniors.
