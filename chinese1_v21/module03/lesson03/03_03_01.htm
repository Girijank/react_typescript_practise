准备好了吗？Zhǔnbèi hǎole ma? Ready to Go?

Excellent job moving along learning how to introduce and describe your family members. Wonderful! Let’s not stop right here, and let’s explore more vocabulary and structures on the topic. Now, imagine you just made a new friend online who is from abroad. How are you going to describe your family to him or her, and how will you comment on the family pictures he or she may show you? Also, how are you two going to discuss what countries or regions you are from, using the Chinese language? In this lesson, we’ll tackle them all.
