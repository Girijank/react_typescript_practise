你的样子 Nǐde yàngzi Your Looks

Have you ever heard of a song called 你的样子 Nǐde yàngzi (Your Looks)? It is a pop song sung by singer 罗大佑 Luó Dàyòu (Tayou Lo) from Taiwan. Its poetic and philosophical lyrics describe how we can change our looks and perspectives of the world over time, while some things never change.

By now you’ve learned how to describe one’s appearance, such as height, hair color, and eye color. You’ve also learned how to describe one’s personality, such as outgoing and shy. Now let’s add some more vocabulary to such topics.

打个卡 Dǎgè kǎ Checkpoint

There is a Chinese saying 水滴石穿 shuǐ dī shí chuān . (Drops of water can pierce a stone.) Constant effort can bring success. Let’s keep the drops of water going until the stone is pierced.
